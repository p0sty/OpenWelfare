This page contains the process to be followed when a member of a couple dies and the deceased partner was not in receipt of income support payments at the time of death.

Notification of death of a partner not receiving payments

Step

Action

1

Agency notified of death + Read more ...

When a person tells Services Australia about the death of a customer:

  • if the person providing the information is distressed, offer a private interview before proceeding
  • offer the pamphlet Needing help after someone has died? (HD001). If accepted, issue using the Mail Forms workflow or guided procedure or manually in person
  • offer the services of a social worker

The following cultural issues need to be respected and considered by Service Officers when being advised of the death of an Indigenous customer:

  • the name of the deceased person cannot be mentioned by immediate family members. This means immediate family members may have to ask other relatives/friends to call on their behalf or attend the interview with the notifier, because they cannot say the name of the deceased
  • in this situation, it may also be offensive to the person for the Service Officer to say the name of the deceased customer, if unsure, Service Officers should sensitively ask the person if it is all right for them to use the deceased customer's name. If the person is not comfortable with this, avoid using the deceased customer's name wherever possible. See Cultural Considerations when someone dies

2

Confirm the payment + Read more ...

Is the surviving partner in receipt of Parenting Payment (PP) as a partnered customer?

  • Yes, see Death of a partner of a Parenting Payment Partnered (PPP) customer
  • No, go to Step 3

3

Confirm the death of the deceased customer + Read more ...

Run the Notification of death guided procedure in the deceased customerâ€™s record to determine if death can be confirmed.

Confirmation of death can be accepted where the full name of the deceased is provided together with two of the following items:

  • deceased customer's last address in full
  • date of birth
  • Customer Reference Number (CRN)
  • name of partner

Confirmation of death also requires the following information about the informant:

  • name
  • address
  • phone number

Confirm if the person notifying of the death is the executor of the estate. If the informant is not the executor, but knows the executor details, obtain:

  • the name and address of the executor, and
  • the name of the funeral director or hospital

Can informant supply enough information to confirm death?

  • Yes, go to Step 5
  • No, go to Step 4

4

Death has not been confirmed + Read more ...

If the death has not been confirmed:

  • use the Notification of death guided procedure to obtain the informant's name, address, contact details and relationship to the deceased. The name of the funeral director (if known) or hospital where the deceased passed away should also be obtained
  • the guided procedure will not code the death, but will generate a DOC which is an open work item to be allocated to a Service Officer with the required skill to complete
  • if the guided procedure is not available, create an open work item. Create a Fast Note, select Auto Text. Use Generic > Update > Death Notification (Adult) - ACTION REQUIRED
  • for Service Officers confirming the death, contact the funeral director or hospital where the deceased passed away. If this is not an option and contact with relatives is proposed, social worker involvement may be necessary. Where appropriate send Request for Verification (SS257) form. However, in these circumstances, it may be most efficient to confirm the death with the relevant Funeral Director

When death is confirmed, go to Step 5

5

Outstanding activities + Read more ...

A death action cannot be processed with activities on the Activity List (AL) screen. The Notification of death guided procedure will prompt if there is an activity on the AL screen.

Where possible ensure any activities are completed and run the daily driver (DDR).

Is there a started activity on the record (which cannot be finalised by the Service Officer) that would prevent finalisation of the death action?

  • Yes, go to Step 6
  • No, go to Step 7

6

When it is not possible to complete an activity + Read more ...

If there is an outstanding activity that the Service Officer is unable to complete, contact the relevant Service Officer or team responsible:

  • if there is a 'STA' debt activity on the AL screen, create an open work item for region XWA advising them of the death and requesting the debt activity be completed. Create a Fast Note, select Auto Text. Use Debts > DMB Raising > Debt Raising ACTion DOC Request. See Requesting refunds and raising debts for deceased customer
  • if there is an RCA/NCL or RCA/ HOM activity on the AL screen, add the keyword RCAPRI. The RCA team will action the activity and complete the death action
  • for other activities, immediately contact the team or Service Officer who needs to complete the outstanding activity - if unsure who to contact to get this activity completed, refer to existing escalation channels

If an open DOC/work item regarding the death notification has not already been created, either

  • run the Notification of death guided procedure to generate an open work item for the relevant staff to complete the death action once the outstanding activities have been completed, or
  • if the guided procedure is not available create an open work item. Create a Fast Note, select Auto Text. Use Generic > Update > Death Notification (Adult) - ACTION REQUIRED

When activity is completed, go to Step 7.

7

Code the death action + Read more ...

Use the Notification of Death guided procedure to code death action (which includes executor details, if known).

If the guided procedure is not available, manually code the death action and record the details of the bereavement on a DOC.

When recording a death, the Notification Handler (NOHL) is incorrectly using the date of notification as the date to increase the survivor's rate to the single rate. The workaround for this is to record the date of receipt (DOR) as the date of death or date paid to + 1 (whichever is the later).

Note: the workaround auto-applies if the Notification of Death guided procedure is used.

8

Advise surviving partner of available assistance + Read more ...

Tell the surviving partner what other assistance Services Australia may be able to provide:

  • Parenting Payment (PP) for single customers who have a qualifying dependent child/ren and who do not have the support of a partner. See: Claiming Parenting Payment (PP)
  • if the customer is in receipt of JobSeeker Payment (JSP), Youth Allowance, Parenting Payment or Special Benefit paid under JSP conditions, they can be exempted from mutual obligation requirements or activity test requirements for the 14 week bereavement period. If the customer is pregnant when their partner died, and the expected date of confinement is more than 14 weeks in the future, the participation exemption is extended for the duration of the pregnancy
  • customers will have their income support payment reassessed to the single rate
  • carers whose care receiver partner has died may be entitled to bereavement assistance, depending on what payments the carer receives. If the carer is in receipt of:
    • both Carer Payment (CP) and CA, the carer is entitled to a bereavement payment for CP, being a continuation of CP for the duration of the bereavement period. However, the carer is not entitled to a Lump Sum Bereavement Payment
    • CA and an income support payment that is not CP, the carer is entitled to a CA bereavement payment
  • if the surviving partner is in receipt of an income support payment and was a joint owner of the couple's assets, full ownership automatically transfers to the surviving partner. However, the system will still only assess that the surviving partner owns 50% of the property. These assets are not updated until the surviving partner advises of the transfer of assets. This may occur when a bereavement review is conducted 16 weeks after the death
