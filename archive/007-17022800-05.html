Specialist teams and region code descriptions

Table 1

Team

Description

Additional Child Care Subsidy (ACCS) Processing Team

The ACCS processing team manage claims and referrals for:

  • ACCS Child Wellbeing
  • ACCS Temporary Financial Hardship
  • ACCS Grandparents
  • ACCS Transition to Work

Approved Care Organisations (Region Code: ACO)

ACO action claims received from Approved Care Organisations only.

See Office Locator and Approved Care Organisations (ACO).

Child Support Unit (Region Code: CSJ)

Centrelink`s Child Support Unit (CSJ) is a liaison team between Centrelink and Child Support, specifically around matters to do with child support, Alignment of Care (AoC) and the impact on a customer's Family Tax Benefit (FTB). This team also actions work items resulting from instances where data transfers between ISIS and CUBA systems do not complete or are incorrect.

Referrals to CSJ are created via Fast Note

For Maintenance related enquiries

Select Auto Text use Families > Enquiry > Maintenance Enquiry.

The 'Maintenance Enquiry' template automatically includes the relevant 'ACTCSU' keyword.

For Alignment of Care related enquiries

Select Auto Text use Families > Enquiry > Alignment of Care.

The 'Alignment of Care' template automatically includes the relevant 'ACTAOC' keyword.

Note: manually add the keyword 'URGENT' to the Fast Note if customer is in severe financial hardship or exceptional circumstances apply.

See Office Notes in Office Locator for more information about the team and the referral process.

Families International Claims (Region Code: FIC)

The FIC team processes family assistance and Parental Leave Pay PPL)/Dad and Partner Pay (DAP) claims for customers who are overseas at the time the claim was made or who indicate they intend to leave Australia within 21 days of making the claim.

Services Officers from the Progress of Claims - Families (Escalations) queue may call 223049 for claim enquiries.

Referrals to FIC are made by adding keyword FICREQ to the activity.

Refer to Office Locator for more information about the team and the referrals process.

Families Reconciliation Team (Region Code: NFP)

For auto reconciliation decisions, the Families Reconciliation team:

  • manages all complex reconciliation enquiries
  • provides SME explanations requested by customers

Referral to NFP is via Fast Note where advised in relevant OB processes. See Office Locator for more information.

Integrated Care Team

(Region Code: INC)

The Integrated Care Team is responsible for:

  • All disputed care changes where the date of event is before 23 May 2018. Specialised staff in Child Support Smart Centres are responsible for change with a date of event (DOV) on or after 23 May 2018
  • All disagreed care changes
  • Resolution of data exchange errors resulting from care decisions made in Integrated Care
  • Families new claims resulting from care changes completed by Integrated Care teams

Work started by an INC Service Officer can be determined by a current Display on Access DOC or Progress DOC or other activity with the INC Service Officer's logon in the position and ADE1, ADE2, BNE1, PARRA1, PARRA2 or PERTH1 in the work group.

If a customer calls and work is being managed by an INC Service Officer then attempt to transfer the customer to the Service Officer's full phone number.

See the notes in Office Locator for more information about INC and the referral process.

Note: all disputed care and actual care in special circumstances are managed by appropriate skilled Senior Service Officers in Child Support Smart Centres.

Manual Intervention Team
(Region Code: MIV)

The Manual Intervention team is responsible for all manual intervention activities which result from reconciliation. Some of their tasks result from activities that have been created throughout the year for example, bereavement cases, use of Payment Adjustment (PYAJ) screen, blended families, Rate Component (RAC) or screen adjustments. Their task is to confirm if these need to be included in reconciliation.

MIV activities must not be cancelled. If a customer contacts regarding their reconciliation or an update cannot be completed due to an outstanding MIV activity, refer to MIV using the Fast Note. Select Auto Text use Families > Reconciliation > Outstanding MIV activity to send details to region code MIV, with the keyword 'ACTMIV' added.

If the customer is at risk or experiencing hardship, add the keyword 'URGENT'.

See Office Locator and Manual intervention into family assistance reconciliation about MIV and the referral process.

National ATO Liaison (Region Code: TAP)

This team is the liaison point between Centrelink and the Australian Taxation Office (ATO), where reconciliation has not occurred due to data exchange issues.

Referrals to TAP are via Fast Note where advised in relevant OB processes.

See Australian Taxation Office (ATO) information not received for family assistance reconciliation or lump sum claim and Office Locator.

Paid Parental Leave Employer Processing Team (Region Code: PEP)

The PPL Employer Processing Team (PPLEPT) assists employers to meet their obligations in providing Parental Leave Pay to their eligible employees.

Referrals to PPLEPT are via Fast Note where advised in relevant OB processes with keyword 'ACTPEP'. Employers can contact PPLEPT on 131 158 (this number is not to be given to customers).

See Office Locator for more information about the team and the referral process.

Smart Centres Families Bereavement and Double Orphan Pension Team (Region Code: DOP)

The Smart Centres Families Bereavement and Double Orphan Pension team is responsible for assessing:

  • Double Orphan Pension (DOP) claims and reviews
  • Stillborn Baby Payment claims
  • Bereavement claims (FTB and PPL) and activities for both stillbirths and older children

Customers can contact the DOP help desk on free call 1800 118 388.

Referrals to DOP team are via Fast Note where advised in relevant OB processes with keyword 'FAOBRV' or 'ACTDOP'. See Office Locator for more information about referrals to DOP in Office Messages > Office Notes.
