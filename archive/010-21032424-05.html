Table 1

Item

Description

1

Student completes year 12 or equivalent study in the same year they commence their tertiary studies

Cathy is 19 and has completed the equivalent of year 12 studies at the end of Semester 1 2022.

Cathy lodges a TAP online claim in July 2022 as she has enrolled in a TAP approved course commencing in Semester 2 2022.

Cathy is eligible for the TAP as she has commenced her tertiary course within 12 months immediately following completion of year 12 or equivalent and has met all other eligibility criteria.

2

Student completes year 12 or equivalent study in a different year from commencing their tertiary studies

Deb is 20 and has completed the equivalent of year 12 studies at the end of Semester 1 2021.

Deb lodges a TAP online claim in January 2022 as she has enrolled in a TAP approved course commencing in Semester 1 2022.

Deb is eligible for the TAP as she has commenced her tertiary course within 12 months immediately following completion of year 12 or equivalent and has met all other eligibility criteria.

3

Student successfully completes year 12 twice

Nichole is 19 and has lodged a TAP claim in January 2022 stating she will commence her TAP approved course in Semester 1 2022.

Nichole states in her TAP online claim that she has successfully completed year 12 at secondary school at the end of Semester 2 2020 and has also successfully completed an equivalent of year 12 studies at TAFE at the end of Semester 2 2021.

Nichole is not eligible for TAP as she is to be assessed on her first successful completion of year 12, in this instance this would be her secondary school studies completed at the end of Semester 2 2020.
