Medical report/carer questionnaire for CP claim cancels CA - Adult care receiver

Table 1

Scenario

Action

Outcome

Carer claiming CP receives CA for the adult.

Make sure all permanent conditions have been covered on the latest medical report.

Cancel CA from the date of determination.

Go to the:

  • care receivers record to record the new medical report/carer questionnaire outside of the claim from the determination date and finalise the activity
  • carers record to cancel CA from the date of determination. DOC the record
  • care receivers record and correct the effect date of the medical report/carer questionnaire to the DOE of the new claim and finalise the activity
  • claimants record and reject the claim

Carer claiming CP and another carer received CA for the adult.

Make sure all permanent conditions have been covered on the latest medical report.

Cancel CA for other carer after contact. See Advising verbally of an unfavourable decision.

Go to the:

  • care receivers record to record the new medical report/carer questionnaire outside of the claim from the determination date and finalise the activity
  • current carers record to cancel CA from the date of determination. DOC the record
  • back to care receivers record and correct the effect date of the medical report/carer questionnaire to the DOE of the new claim and finalise the activity
  • claimants record and reject the claim

Only one form lodged.

Check if the reuse provisions apply.

Reuse provisions apply:

  • process the CP claim

Reuse provisions do not apply:

  • do not record the new medical report/carer questionnaire
  • cancel CA from the date of determination
  • reject the CP claim - FRC

Table 2

Scenario

Action

Outcome

The care receiver has a recognised disability, which is of a permanent nature, but the condition(s) is not listed on the current medical report.

Carer claiming CP receives CA for the child or another carer receives CA for the child.

Clarify with the Treating Health Professional (THP) why the recognised disability was not listed on the latest report.

If not confirmed, check DOCs on the carer record, and if needed check the previous report to confirm the condition was correctly recorded.

Continue CA if the recognised disability is confirmed.

Refer to the Level 2 Policy Help Desk if the recognised disability had been correctly recorded and the care receiver no longer has this condition.

Carer claiming CP receives CA for the child.

Clarify with the THP why the recognised disability was not listed on the latest report.

If not confirmed, check DOCs on the carer record and if needed check the previous report to confirm the condition was correctly recorded.

Continue CA if the recognised disability is confirmed.

Otherwise, cancel CA, from the date of determination.

The care receiver has a recognised disability, which is not of a permanent nature, and the condition(s) is not listed on the current medical report.

Another carer receives CA for the child.

Clarify with the THP why the recognised disability was not listed on the latest report.

If not confirmed, check DOCs on the carer record, and if needed check the previous report to confirm the condition was correctly recorded.

Continue CA if the recognised disability is confirmed via a new THP.

Cancel CA for other carer after contact. See Advising verbally of an unfavourable decision.

The care receiver does not have a recognised disability.

  • Cancel CA from the date of determination. DOC the record
  • correct the effect date of the medical report/carer questionnaire to the DOE of the new claim and finalise the activity
  • go to the claimants record and reject the claim

The care receiver does not have a recognised disability.

Another carer receives CA for the child.

Make sure all permanent conditions have been covered on the latest medical report.

Cancel CA for other carer after contact. See Advising verbally of an unfavourable decision.

  • in the carers record code the new medical report/carer questionnaire outside of the claim from the determination date and finalise the activity
  • cancel CA from the date of determination. DOC the record
  • correct the effect date of the medical report/carer questionnaire to the DOE of the new claim and finalise the activity
  • go to the claimants record and reject the claim

Multiple claims lodged

Table 5

Qualifying care receivers

Example

Nil

Customer qualifies for Nil of the care receivers claimed for.

Before rejecting the claims check if any of the following are applicable:

  • Carer Payment (CP) (child) when caring for 2 to 4 children each with a disability or medical condition (combined care)
  • Eligibility for Carer Payment (CP) under the Lower Adult Disability Assessment (ADAT) Tool
  • Carer Payment (CP) (child) when caring for a disabled adult and 1 or 2 children with a disability or medical condition (multiple care)

Rejection is to be completed for both/ all receivers claimed for.

Carer has lodged CP claim for more than one care receiver

  • Multiple CP claim is to be made 'Not required'
  • SOA is to be created for each care receiver and 'Rejected' one at a time

Carer is current on CP

  • Claim for the new care receiver is to be made 'Not required'
  • Claim is considered 'Rejected'
  • Review CP in respect of the existing care receiver

One

Carer has lodged CP claim for more than one care receiver and qualifies for one of the care receivers claimed for.

  • Original claim is to be made 'Not required'
  • Create SOA and 'Reject' for the care receiver/s that do not qualify
  • Create SOA and 'Grant' for the qualifying care receiver

More than one

Customer:

  • qualifies for more than one care receivers claimed for, or
  • is in receipt of CP, and qualifies for CP for the new care receiver claimed for

Contact customer to confirm care receiver being claimed for.

Carer has lodged CP claim for more than one care receiver

  • Withdraw claim for multiple care receivers
  • Process claim for care receiver being claimed for

Carer is current on CP

  • Withdraw claim for new care receiver, or
  • Process change in care receiver (cancelling original care receiver and processing claim for new care receiver)
