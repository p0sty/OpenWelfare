Scenarios

Table 2

Scenario

Date of first (arrears) payment and amount - payment is made on a Friday

Date of second or subsequent (weekly) payment

A customer was granted DRA on 3 March 2022 for an income loss date that started on 28 February 2022.

The customer:

  • had received payments of DRA before 8 April 2022
  • has a residential address and works in the local government area (LGA) of Lismore

8 April 2022

The customer will be paid 5 weeks of DRA (Top-up) on 8 April 2022 at $350 per week of payment = $1,750

15 April 2022 and weekly thereafter until the customer’s DRA entitlement ends

A customer was granted DRA on 4 April 2022 for an income loss date that started on 2 April 2022.

The customer:

  • received their first payment of DRA on 9 April 2022
  • has a residential address in the LGA of Lismore

15 April 2022

The customer is not paid on 8 April as at least 7 days have not passed since the customer’s income loss date

The customer will be paid 1 week of DRA (Top-up) on 15 April 2022 at $350 per week of payment = $350

22 April and weekly thereafter until the customer’s DRA entitlement ends

A customer was granted DRA on 22 April 2022 for an income loss date that started on 18 April 2022.

The customer:

  • received their first payment of DRA on 22 April 2022
  • has a residential address in the LGA of Lismore

29 April 2022

22 April is a Friday and payments of the DRA (Top-up) would have already been paid on this day.

The next payment day for the DRA (Top-up) is Friday 29 April 2022

The customer will be paid 1 week of DRA (Top-up) on 29 April 2022 at $350 per week of payment = $350

6 May 2022 and weekly thereafter until the customer’s DRA entitlement ends

A customer’s loss of income is 22 February 2022. They received a payment of DRA on 16 March 2022 then cancelled on 17 March 2022. The customer was current for 23 days.

The customer has a residential address in the LGA of Lismore

8 April 2022

The customer will be paid 4 weeks of DRA (Top-up) on 8 April 2022 at $350 per week of payment = $1,400

This is because if a customer meets the eligibility criteria, the DRA (Top-up) is a payment of $350 per week, for each week (or part thereof) that they are eligible for DRA or NZ DRA for the New South Wales Floods - February 2022.

Does not apply as the customer’s DRA entitlement has ended
