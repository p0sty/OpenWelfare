Adding a Manual Partner Intervention 110-13060050

  • Background
  • Process Summary
  • Process
Expand all


For staff conducting Customer Compliance Division Interventions only

This document outlines the process for adding manual partner interventions within Customer Compliance Division.

Partner intervention identified

If during the intervention process, it is identified that the intervention outcome could have an impact on a partner's (or ex-partner's) entitlement (current or past) a manual partner intervention must be created on the partner's/ex partner's record.

This will ensure all updates made to the customer's and partner's/ex-partner's records are attributed to the intervention results.

A manual partner intervention should not be loaded if the partner/ex-partner:

  • was not in receipt of a Centrelink payment during the period of the intervention
  • has a primary intervention in their own right

Note: this process does not apply to Employment Income Matching (NEIM) Interventions
