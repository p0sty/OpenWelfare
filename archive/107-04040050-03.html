For staff responsible for raising debts only

This page contains information on the special arrangements for recovering debts raised through the International Data Exchange Programme.

Foreign Pension Data Exchange debt recovery

Recovering debts raised through the International Data Exchange Programme

This table describes the steps to follow when recovering debts raised through the International Data Exchange Programme.

Step

Action

1

Non-standard recovery of debts raised under the International Data Exchange Programme + Read more ...

Normal procedures for raising debts and contacting customers apply to debts raised under the International Data Exchange Programme. Customers may also contact with regard to the existing recovery of debts.

Is there a debt as a result of income adjusted through the International Data Exchange Programme?

  • Yes, go to Step 2
  • No, see Debt recovery for Centrelink. Procedure ends here

2

Does the customer have any other debts? + Read more ...

  • Yes, go to Step 3
  • No, go to Step 5

3

Customer has other debts + Read more ...

Are withholdings currently in place under the special arrangements for recovery of debts from International Data Exchange Programme debts (that is, $50 pf)?

  • Yes, no further action is required. Procedure ends here
  • No, go to Step 4

4

Are withholdings currently in place? + Read more ...

  • Yes, during the contact with the customer, advise them the current arrangement for repaying their existing debt will continue towards repayment of the International Data Exchange Programme debt
    • If they are happy with this arrangement to continue for the International Data Exchange Programme debt, record all details on a DOC. Procedure ends here
    • If they are not happy with this arrangement, create a manual review for when the other debts will be repaid to change the withholding rate to $50pf for the recovery of the income from Foreign Pension Data Exchange debt. Procedure ends here
  • No, go to Step 5

5

Withholding rate + Read more ...

Special recovery arrangements are in place for International Data Exchange Programme debts. The recovery rate must be set to $50pf, unless the customer wishes to pay more.

During the contact with the customer, discuss the special withholding rate for the recovery of their International Data Exchange Programme debt.

The customer can choose to pay more, but this must be at their request.

Has the customer advised that they are unable to afford $50pf or do they wish to discuss making an alternative arrangement?

  • Yes, refer the case to the Debt Management Branch. Procedure ends here
  • No, go to Step 6

6

Code withholding rate + Read more ...

  • Select the debt from the Debt List (OPDL) screen and type 'OPAL' in the Nxt: field and press [Enter]
  • In the Create new arrangement type: field, type 'WHS' and press [Enter]
  • On the Withholding Instruction (OPWH) screen, complete the following fields:
    • Withholding Type: field
    • Non Standard Amount $: field, code '$50' (or the agreed withholding rate if more than this amount)
    • Comments: field, code 'Rate adj to $50pf, International Data Exchange'
    • Press [Enter]
    • Code 'Y' in the Finished?: field on the Assessment Finalisation (OPAF) screen
    • Press [Enter]
    • Note: if the activity is to be Quality on line (QOL) checked, code 'Q' in the Finished?: field

8

Recording details + Read more ...

  • Record details of the special recovery arrangements for income from International Data Exchange Programme debts and the agreed withholding rate, on a Display on Access DOC
  • A letter will automatically be sent advising the amount to be deducted from ongoing payments
