This page contains examples of how the system calculates the benefit for varying account paid amounts when the Medicare safety net has been met, calculations when verification (substantiation) is required and examples when an unpaid account is claimed. It contains a description of transaction codes, examples of when a person is unable to confirm their family composition at a service centre and links to Medicare Safety Net templates and standard letter templates. There are also links to Medicare Safety Net user help guides. Links on this page may be unavailable to external audiences.
