Group assessment service items: (81100, 81110, 81120, 93284, 93286) and
RACF items (93606-93608)

Group treatment service items: (81105, 81115, 81125, 93285) and RACF
items (93613-93615)

RACF additional physical therapy group treatment item (93620)

Note: RACF items ended 30 June 2022. These items will still contribute
to the total number of services for the remainder of the calendar year.
