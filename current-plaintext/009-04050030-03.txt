For Carer Payment/Carer Allowance Smart Centre Processing staff only

This page contains instructions for coding a paper claim for Carer
Payment (CP) for one care receiver or combined care receivers aged under
16 including where the carer is claiming CP due to a change of care
receiver or during the bereavement period. The following is an interim
process subject to change.

Processing Service details for: Carer Allowance/Payment New Claims.

On this page:

Initial processing

Coding medical and care receiver details

Finalising claim
