Risks to a customer's privacy have been identified. See Separating
Safely - protecting personal details to make sure the customer's
personal details are safe before progressing.

This page contains information about obtaining identity confirmation
from parents and carers and the types of checks that may be considered.
This includes identity checks for representatives and the process to
follow if they refuse to provide identity.
