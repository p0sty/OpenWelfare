Scenario

Customer not eligible for exit within 13 weeks of commencing Voluntary
Income Management

Sarah entered into a Voluntary Income Management Agreement at her local
Centrelink Service Centre. Sarah returns a week later and advises the
Service Officer she wants to exit Voluntary Income Management as she
feels she is capable of managing her money on her own.

Sarah is required to remain on Voluntary Income Management for the 13
week mandatory period.

Sarah is advised of her review and appeal rights.
