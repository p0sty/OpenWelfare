This document outlines details of PBS-subsidised biological agents for
patients with ankylosing spondylitis (AS).

AS and listing dates

AS is an inflammatory disease of the joints in the spine.

Listing dates:

-   infliximab - 1 August 2004
-    etanercept - 1 April 2005
-    adalimumab - 1 March 2007
-    golimumab - 1 August 2010
-    certolizumab pegol - 1 September 2014
-    secukinumab - 1 October 2016
-    ixekizumab - 1 December 2020
-    upadacitinib - 1 October 2021
-    tofacitinib - 1 August 2023

See Written Authority Required Drugs for more information.

Treatment specifics

A break in treatment cycles is measured:

-   from the date of last approval for PBS-subsidised biological
    medicine treatment in the most recent cycle
-    to the date of the first application for initial treatment with a
    biological medicine under the new treatment cycle

There is no limit to the number of treatment cycles.

See Treatment breaks and failures for full details.
