This is a Family and Domestic Violence Interaction Point. If the
customer is with another person, on speaker phone, or already identified
with family and domestic violence concerns, continue with the current
business conversation. Otherwise, go to the Family and domestic violence
procedure to conduct the risk identification and referral process.

This page contains the process for assessing eligibility and completing
a transfer from Parenting Payment Single (PPS) to Parenting Payment
Partnered (PPP).

On this page:

Customer advises changes in relationship status

Eligibility to transfer to PPP

Running Assisted Customer Claim (ACC) to initiate a transfer to PPP

Action required for customers not qualified or not pursuing PPP

Finalising the transfer to PPP
