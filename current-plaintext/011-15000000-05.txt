System letters

MCRS issues letters during claim processing. Service Officers must not
use locally produced letters.

Letter recipients may include:

Claimant:

-   injured person (14 years or over and able to manage their own
    affairs)
-   parent, guardian, power of attorney, legal representative, public
    trustee, or
-   an authorised third party. For example, a solicitor

Notifiable person â€“ compensation payer or insurer, or their solicitor or
agent

Standard letter template manual letters should only be used in limited
circumstances. See Using Standard Letter Templates (SLT).
