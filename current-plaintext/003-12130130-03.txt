For Services Australia social workers only.

If a child is in immediate danger or risk of harm you must act, and a
delegate must call 000.If a child is in immediate danger or risk of harm
you must act, and a delegate must call 000.

This page contains the procedure for Services Australia Social Workers
to record Vulnerable Welfare Payment Recipient (VWPR) Youth Exclusion
(Detrimental) assessments in the Social Work Information System (SWIS).

On this page:

Process Direct

Customer First
