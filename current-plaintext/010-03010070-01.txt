This document outlines information on the Assistance for Isolated
Children (AIC) Marginal Distance Confirmation (SY069). This is completed
by an appropriate authority to establish entitlement to AIC where the
distance or the travel time of the journey from a student's principal
family home to the nearest state school is close to the qualifying
limits for AIC.

Completing the SY069

In these situations, applicants may be requested to have the SY069
completed by one of the following authorities:

-   local bus company proprietor
-   local shire clerk/engineer
-   local/state/national roads, water and/or land management authority

The applicant needs to complete Questions 1 to 5.

The appropriate authority is to complete the remaining questions,
including the declaration which requires all of the following:

-   the authority's name
-   the staff member's name, contact phone number and signature
-   the date

Returning the SY069

The form can be posted to the relevant AIC Smart Centre using the
Customer Postal Address on the relevant Office Locator page and
addressing the mail to 'Student Services' (this address is also shown on
the form). Otherwise, it may be lodged online or at any Service Centre
for processing by the AIC Smart Centre.

The Resources page contains a link to the SY069 and to Office Locator.
