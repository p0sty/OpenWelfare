This page contains how the income and asset details contained in the
Income and Assets (SA369) are used to assess a customer's entitlement to
an Income Support Payment (ISP). For Farm Household Allowance (FHA), see
Assessing assets for Farm Household Allowance (FHA) or Assessing income
for Farm Household Allowance.

(SA369)
