Collection, storage and use of personal information

Information on the collection, storage and limits on the use of personal
information is found in the Australian Privacy Principles (APP).
Principle 3 provides information on obtaining personal details from the
recipient.

To view Principle 3 - collection of solicited personal information, see
the Privacy Act 1998, Schedule 1, Australian Privacy Principles
