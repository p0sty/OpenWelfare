This document outlines details of PBS-subsidised avatrombopag,
eltrombopag and romiplostim for patients with severe chronic immune
idiopathic thrombocytopenic purpura (ITP).

ITP and listing dates

ITP is a condition with isolated low platelet counts and an increased
tendency for patients to bleed.

Listing dates:

-   romiplostim - 1 April 2011
-   eltrombopag - 1 November 2011
-   avatrombopag - 1 July 2023

See Written Authority Required Drugs for more information.

Enquiries

Transfer enquiries about prescription arrangements to the PBS Complex
Drugs Program team and choose the option relevant to the condition
treated.
