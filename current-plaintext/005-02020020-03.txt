This page contains the manual referral process to Centrelink
International Services (CIS) for NARWP or qualifying residence
exemptions due to being a family member of a refugee or former refugee,
and information to assist with these assessments. For information on how
the Newly Arrived Resident's Waiting Period (NARWP) and Qualifying
Residence Exemptions (QRE) were impacted by coronavirus (COVID-19), see
the Resources page.
