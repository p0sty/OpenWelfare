Processing MFUs

The information below explains the required action for each aged care
Manual Follow-up (MFU).

Match the 2 line information message on the Activity List (AL) screen
with the list of MFUs below. For ease of access, the information is
divided into the action that has produced the MFU, that is, entries and
discharges.

Note: while references to DSS (Department of Social Security) and DHA
(the former Department of Health and Aged Care and Ageing) in steps 1 to
4 of Table 2 are out of date, they reflect the text that currently
appears in the automatically created MFUs.
