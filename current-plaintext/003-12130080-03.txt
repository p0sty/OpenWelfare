
Recording unreasonable to live at home (UTLAH) assessments for customers under 18 years of age - social work 003-12130080

-   Background
-   Process

[Expand/Collapse all sections]Expand all

If a child is in immediate danger or risk of harm you must act, and a
delegate must call 000.

For Services Australia social workers only.

This page contains recording Unreasonable to Live at Home (UTLAH)
assessments in the Social Work Recording Application (SWRA) in Process
Direct and the Social Work Information System (SWIS) in Customer First.
