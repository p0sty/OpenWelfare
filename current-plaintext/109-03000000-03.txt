This page contains information to help Service Officers when customers
make initial contact about a Centrelink decision. A separate procedure
applies if the customer has already requested an explanation, quality
check or formal review of the decision.

On this page:

Service Officer contact guide

Customer has further information

Customer has lodged an Explanation or formal review of a decision form
(SS351 or SY054)
