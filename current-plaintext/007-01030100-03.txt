This page contains information on how to help Parents who want to claim
Parenting Payment (PP).

Select the relevant tab:

-   [] Self service tab explains how to help a person claim online
-   Assisted tab explains how to check a parents circumstances to ensure
    a new claim is required, completing an assisted customer claim with
    a customer/nominee, and lodgement of paper forms
