For processing staff only

This page contains the instructions on how to process the Manual
Identity Verification Provider Digital Access (HW080) form for a user.

On this page:

Locating the correct PRODA account and checking the form and required
identity documents

Add Evidence of Identity documentation

Add evidence of change of name

Peer review and manually overriding documents
