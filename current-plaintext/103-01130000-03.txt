For National Business Gateway Income Management Processing staff

This page contains information relating to actioning a Rent Deduction
Scheme (RDS) instruction. This process excludes deduction for Northern
Territory Housing as these requests are managed using a separate
process, specific to Northern Territory Housing.

On this page:

State Housing Authority (HA) requested Rent Deduction Scheme (RDS)
instruction

Rent Deduction Scheme (RDS) requests for SA, WA or NSW

Rent Deduction Scheme (RDS) requests for VIC, TAS, ACT or QLD
