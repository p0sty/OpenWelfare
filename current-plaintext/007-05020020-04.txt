Links to the Federal Register of Legislation site goes to a 'Series'

The A New Tax System (Family Assistance) Act 1999 defines an Australian
resident as having the same definition as the Social Security Act 1991.
However, section 21 of the A New Tax System (Family Assistance) Act 1999
also states that a person with a Special Category Visa (SCV) residing in
Australia or a person holding a visa qualifying them for Special Benefit
(SpB) may also be eligible for Family Tax Benefit (FTB).
