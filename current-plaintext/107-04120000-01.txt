This document outlines what action to take with debt activities created
by Service Updates.

Service Profiling debts

Services Australia reports debts created by Service Profiling to Policy
Departments.

Payment Assurance Operations Teams are responsible for all non-Customer
Compliance Division Service Update debt functions.

The Resources page contains information on how the Debt Action script
combines multiple debts and how users can manually record Attribution of
the debt if the script is not available.
