Delegations and authorisations

Child Support Services, Delegations and Authorisations

Service Officer (SO3)

SO3 authorisations:

-   process an application for an extension of child support for
    secondary students
-   relevant decision making

Note: this does not prevent an officer at the Service Officer (SO2)
level who has the support of their team leader from carrying out this
type of work. However, the final decision to either accept or refuse the
application must be by an officer at the SO3 level or above.

Service Officer (SO4) and above

SO4 and above authorisations:

-   accept or refuse a late application (after child's 18^(th) birthday)
    for assessment/agreement to continue in exceptional circumstances
