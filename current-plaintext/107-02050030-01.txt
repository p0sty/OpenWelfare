Guidelines

Centrelink Issues for Resolution (CIFR) provide guidelines due to
cross-area consolidation of functions. These guidelines provide details
of how to map debts arising from the activity of consolidated teams.

The Resources page contains a link to contact details for the Debt and
Compensation Program (DCP) Branch and Payment Assurance Operations
(PAO).
