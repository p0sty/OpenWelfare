This page contains information about processing a notice of judgment or
settlement for an existing case in the judgment/settlement work step in
the Compensation Case Management System (CCMS).

Note: steps included in the tables assume scanning, mail validation and
mandatory checks have already been completed in accordance with the
latest advice provided to Service Officers. Ask a Team Leader if unsure
of the current procedures for scanning, mail validation and/or mandatory
checks.
