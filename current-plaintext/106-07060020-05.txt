COVID-19 affected

A customer is determined to be COVID-19 affected if they are:

-   in a COVID-19 affected area, see Local outbreaks
-   in mandatory self-isolation
-   awaiting results of a COVID-19 test and are required to self isolate
-   experiencing COVID -19 symptoms and have been informed by a required
    authority to self-isolate or quarantine
-   unable to access POI documents due to border closures
