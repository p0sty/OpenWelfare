This page contains details to help Employer Services Officers (ESO) set
up withholding/employer withholding of arrears (EW/EWA), give advice to
Services Officers, maintain EW links and amend or end EW.

On this page:

Review link referral

Contact with employer to set up EW

Follow up action after employer contact

Update employer details

Finalise linking request in Cuba

Amend or end an existing EW link
