Verbal scripts for Assisted Customer Claims (ACC)

Item

Description

1

Verbal declaration script

Read the following to customers when they make a request under sections 1136 and 1137AA of the Social Security Act. Customers must accept the below details and the terms and conditions of the HEAS.

  • The information in this application for loan payments under the Pension Loans Scheme which is known as the Home Equity Access Scheme (HEAS) is complete and correct
  • If declarant making a request: This request is made under section <If declarant claiming fortnightly: 1136> <If declarant claiming both: and> <if declarant claiming advance: 1137AA> of the Social Security Act 1991 to participate in the HEAS
  • You will notify Centrelink of any changes to this information within fourteen (14) days of the change(s) occurring
  • You have read and understand the conditions of the HEAS
  • If declarant’s partner making a request: You agree to <partner> making a request under section <If partner claiming fortnightly: 1136> <If partner claiming both: and> <if partner claiming advance: 1137AA> of the Social Security Act 1991 to participate in the HEAS.>
  • If partnered: You agree to the information in this application being made available to your partner so they can decide whether to agree to the application
  • You have read the terms and conditions for Electronic Messaging and online letters if you have provided a mobile phone number or email address
  • You have read and understand the Privacy Notice

2

Verbal acknowledgement script

This text details the conditions and presents to the customer as they make the declaration. Written consent is still collected from customer and partner.

  • You may need to provide further information if requested
  • Giving false or misleading information is a serious offence
  • Centrelink can make relevant enquiries to ensure you receive my correct entitlement
  • Any information you provide on this application may be used to determine eligibility and suitability for services for me and (where relevant) my partner and any third parties
  • You need to advise Centrelink of any changes to your circumstances that may affect participation on the HEAS within fourteen (14) days of the change(s) occurring
  • Loan payments cannot start until this statement is accepted and all supporting documentation is provided to Centrelink
  • Interest will be charged on the outstanding balance of my loan and compounded fortnightly until the debt is fully repaid
  • Loan payments advanced to you will be reduced from the maximum of any fortnightly loan payments you would get for the next 26 fortnights and you may get less loan payment each fortnight
  • You understand you can only take out two loan advances in any 26 fortnight period
  • The nominated property will be taken as security against the loan and a charge or a caveat will be registered against the title of this property, with any costs incurred by the Commonwealth associated with registering or removing this charge or caveat being payable by me in accordance with section 1143 of the Social Security Act 1991.
  • You can change the rate of loan payments you receive under the HEAS at any time by request to Centrelink
  • You may terminate receipt of fortnightly loan payments by request to Centrelink, and either repay the loan in full or leave the outstanding balance, including accruing interest, to be recovered when the property is sold or from my estate after my death.
  • Your fortnightly loan payments will cease once the maximum loan amount is reached
  • You must notify Centrelink if you intend to change the title details or dispose of a property secured for the purpose of this loan
  • Your partner needs to provide their agreement for your application for payments under the HEAS. If agreement is not received then your claim will be rejected
  • You agree to the information within this application being made available to your partner so they can decide whether to agree to the application
  • You understand if you fail to comply with the conditions of the loan, misrepresent your circumstances or commit fraud then the No Negative Equity Guarantee may not apply to your loan when you go to repay it
  • You understand the No Negative Equity Guarantee only applies when you settle the loan in full and any voluntary repayments you make may reduce the benefit of the No Negative Equity Guarantee
  • Your receipt of payments may be affected if you do not do any of the above
