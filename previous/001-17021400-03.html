This page contains the instructions for Service Officers and authorised PaTH Internship Programme processing staff to follow when job seekers commence in the PaTH Internship Programme.

PaTH Internship Programme and PaTH Incentive (PII) payment

Table 1: This table describes Service Officer actions required if Services Australia is advised that a participant has commenced the PaTH Internship Programme.

Step

Action

1

PaTH Internship Programme + Read more ...

Employment Services Providers are responsible for identifying customers who would benefit from the PaTH Internship Programme.

Service Officers are not required to independently verify or confirm participation in the PaTH Internship Programme.

If a customer contacts Services Australia about the PaTH Internship Programme:

  • advise them to speak to their Employment Services Provider if they have any questions about starting, ceasing or participating in the PaTH Internship programme
  • explain they are not required to notify Services Australia when they commence or cease in the PaTH Internship Programme, and
  • that Services Australia will commence paying the PII payment once confirmation is received from the Department of Education, Skills and Employment

If the Department of Education, Skills and Employment notifies Services Australia a participant has commenced in the PaTH Internship Programme, go to Step 2.

Note: Service Officers must not offer to advocate or contact an Employment Services Provider on the participant's behalf.

Procedure ends here.

2

Department of Education, Skills and Employment notification + Read more ...

The Department of Education, Skills and Employment notifies Services Australia when a participant commences in or ceases the PaTH Internship Programme. This is an automated process.

Details of the referrals are on the Referral Summary screen. Select the PaTH Internship referral to view the:

  • Referral Effective Date
  • Due Date

3

Taxable PaTH Internship Incentive (PII) payment + Read more ...

The taxable PII payment is paid by Services Australia:

  • automatically for JobSeeker Payment (JSP), Youth Allowance (other), Special Benefit or Parenting Payment Single customers, or
  • manually for customers in receipt of Disability Support Pension (DSP)

Is the customer in receipt of DSP?

  • Yes, go to Step 1 in the Manually issue PII payment for Disability Support Pension (DSP) customers table. Note: this process is completed by authorised PaTH Internship Programme processing staff only
  • No, go to Step 4

4

Taxable PII payment effect on incomes and child support debt recovery + Read more ...

For both Centrelink and Child Support purposes, the PII payment cannot be garnisheed directly from Centrelink to address a child support debt.

Child Support Smart Centre (CSSC) staff will need to ensure any PII payment received by a participant is included in their Adjusted Taxable Income (ATI) when taking an estimate of income or updating a provisional income.

CSSC staff should not seek to garnishee this amount from Centrelink. However, once paid, the amount may be garnisheed from a participantâ€™s bank account in line with current debt collection methodology.

Procedure ends here.

Manually issue PII payment for Disability Support Pension (DSP) customers

Table 2: This table describes how to manually issue taxable PII payments for DSP customers. Manual PII payments are actioned by authorised PaTH Internship Programme processing staff only.

Step

Action

1

Notification of participation received + Read more ...

When Services Australia is notified by Department of Education, Skills and Employment that a DSP customer has commenced in the PaTH Internship Programme, the PII payment will be manually issued by authorised PaTH Internship Programme processing staff.

Service Officers are not required to independently verify or confirm the customer's participation in the PaTH Internship Programme.

Using the Payment Adjustment (PYAJ) screen, authorised PaTH Internship Programme processing staff must issue the taxable PII payment manually for every instalment period until the last instalment period of participation in the PaTH Internship Programme.

Note: authorised staff must not issue a PII payment manually for customers in receipt of JobSeeker Payment (JSP), Youth Allowance (other), Special Benefit or Parenting Payment.

Email the Mutual obligation requirements Section for queries about PII payments for DSP participants

2

Accessing the Payment Adjustment Warning (PAWS) screen + Read more ...

Go to the PYAJ screen in the PEN system. This will display the Payment Adjustment Warning (PAWS) screen.

Note: access to the PYAJ screen is restricted and PII payments should only be actioned by an authorised PaTH Internship Programme processing Service Officer. If the screen cannot be accessed, notify Operational Planning Branch so the task can be reallocated to an authorised staff member.

3

PAWS screen + Read more ...

The following information will display:

  • Warning: use of the PYAJ screen is monitored and any payments issued using this screen must be documented
  • key S to view help
  • key Y in the Do you want to continue? field and press [Enter] to continue

4

PYAJ screen - coding the payment + Read more ...

The PYAJ screen will now display. Complete the fields as follows:

  • Reason Code, key SYL (System Limitation)
  • Service Reason, key DSP
  • Period From Date. If the jobseeker:
    • has commenced a new PaTH Internship, use the Referral Effective Date + 5 days, for example if the Referral Effective Date is 2 April 2017, then record 7 April 2017
    • is continuing an existing PaTH Internship, use the customer's Entitlement Period Start Date (EPSD)
  • Period To Date, key the job seeker's Entitlement Period End Date (EPED)
  • Component, key PII
  • Amount, key $200
  • Reason for Payment, key manual coding of PII payment required for DSP customer
  • Source, key FRE
  • Date of Receipt, key [current system date]

Press [Enter] to continue.

5

Payment Adjustment Confirmation (PYAJC) screen + Read more ...

When the PYAJC screen is displayed, confirm the details of the payment are correct.

In the Submit? (Y/N/Q) field, key Y.

6

Finalise activity + Read more ...

Carefully check the Assessment Results (AR) before finalising any activity. Record details of payment made on a DOC as follows:

  • Extra Details, PII payment issued
  • Text, Services Australia has been advised by the Department of Education, Skills and Employment that the customer is undertaking a PaTH Internship from <start date> to <end date> and is eligible for a PII payment. PII payment has been manually issued, as per Operational Blueprint procedure PaTH Internship Programme. If the customer contacts and wishes to know more about this payment advise them to contact their Employment Services Provider
