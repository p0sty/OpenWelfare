This page contains a table showing the benefits payable for other ABSTUDY awards, links to the full eligibility requirements and what is required if the recipient is transferring from the ABSTUDY Part-time award to another ABSTUDY Award.

A student in receipt of the ABSTUDY Part-time Award is eligible for Incidentals Allowance, Fares Allowance and Away From Base assistance.

ABSTUDY Awards eligibility requirements

Item

ABSTUDY Award Requirements

1

Schooling A Award + Read more ...

Age

Under 16 years of age

Type of study

  • full-time primary (where student is 14 years of age or older at 1 January in the year of study)
  • full time secondary

Benefits payable

  • Away From Base assistance or assistance with travel, meals and accommodation costs for distance education/correspondence students to attend residential schools
  • Fares Allowance
  • School Fees Allowance
  • School Term Allowance

Eligibility requirements

ABSTUDY eligibility criteria for Schooling A Award

Lodgement Requirements when transferring from Part-time Award

Claim for ABSTUDY Schooling A (SY018) is required. For further information, see:

Completing the Claim for ABSTUDY Schooling A (SY018)

Assessing the claim for ABSTUDY Schooling A (SY018)

2

Schooling B Award + Read more ...

Age

  • 16 and over, or
  • under 16 IF
    • approved for Away from home or
    • independent, or
    • in State Care

Type of study

  • full-time primary
  • full-time secondary

Benefits payable

  • Away From Base assistance
  • Fares Allowance
  • Incidentals Allowance (where student is 18 years or over at 1 January in the year of study)
  • Living Allowance
  • Pensioner Education Supplement
  • Pharmaceutical Allowance (PhA)
  • Remote Area Allowance (RAA)
  • Rent Assistance
  • School Fees Allowance
  • U16 Boarding Supplement
  • Additional Assistance
  • Relocation Scholarship
  • Student Start-up Loan

Eligibility requirements

ABSTUDY eligibility criteria for Schooling B Award

Lodgement Requirements when transferring from Part-time Award

Claim for ABSTUDY (SY019) is required. The ABSTUDY Additional Information (SY023) is also acceptable along with a MOD JY or MOD P if the student is dependent or has a partner. For further information, see:

  • Completing the Claim for ABSTUDY (SY019)
  • Assessing a claim for ABSTUDY Schooling B Award, Tertiary Award and Masters and Doctorate Award
  • Completing the ABSTUDY Additional Information Form (SY023)
  • Assessing the ABSTUDY Additional Information Form (SY023)

3

Tertiary Award + Read more ...

Age

  • minimum school leaving age, or
  • have an exemption to enter tertiary study

Type of study

Full-time at

  • tertiary institution
  • TAFE
  • private provider
  • university

Benefits payable

  • Additional Incidentals Allowance
  • Away From Base assistance
  • Fares Allowance
  • Incidentals Allowance
  • Living Allowance
  • Pensioner Education Supplement (PES)
  • Pharmaceutical Allowance
  • Remote Area Allowance
  • Rent Assistance
  • Additional Assistance
  • Relocation Scholarship
  • Student Start-up Loan

Eligibility requirements

ABSTUDY eligibility criteria for Tertiary Award

Lodgement Requirements when transferring from Part-time Award

Claim for ABSTUDY (SY019) is required, plus Proof of Enrolment. The ABSTUDY Additional Information (SY023) is also acceptable along with a MOD JY or MOD P if the student is dependent or has a partner. For further information, see:

  • Completing the Claim for ABSTUDY (SY019)
  • Assessing a claim for ABTUDY Schooling B Award, Tertiary Award and Masters and Doctorate Award
  • Completing the ABSTUDY Additional Information Form (SY023)
  • Assessing the ABSTUDY Additional Information Form (SY023)

4

Testing and Assessment Award + Read more ...

Age

  • Minimum school leaving age, or
  • have an exemption to attend TAFE/tertiary institution

Type of study

tertiary institutions

Benefits payable

  • Fares Allowance
  • Away From Base assistance

Eligibility requirements

ABSTUDY eligibility criteria for Testing and Assessment Award

Lodgement Requirements when transferring from Part-time Award

A Claim for ABSTUDY Allowances and Part-time Award (SY021) is required. For further information, see:

  • Completing the Claim for ABSTUDY Allowances and Part-time Award (SY021)
  • Assessing a claim for ABSTUDY Allowances and Part-time Award

5

Masters and Doctorate Award + Read more ...

Age

  • minimum school leaving age, or
  • have an exemption to attend TAFE/tertiary institution

Type of study

tertiary institutions

Benefits payable

  • Additional Incidentals Allowance
  • Away From Base assistance
  • Assistance to pay Student Contribution (previously Higher Education Contribution Scheme (HECS)) commitment or compulsory course fees
  • Fares Allowance or Relocation Allowance
  • Incidentals Allowance
  • Living Allowance
  • Pensioner Education Supplement (PES)
  • Relocation Allowance or Fares Allowance
  • Thesis Allowance
  • Additional Assistance
  • Relocation Scholarship
  • Student Start-up Loan

Eligibility requirements

ABSTUDY eligibility criteria for Masters and Doctorate Award

Lodgement Requirements when transferring from Part-time Award

Claim for ABSTUDY (SY019) is required, plus Proof of Enrolment. The ABSTUDY Additional Information Form (SY023) is also acceptable along with a MOD JY or MOD P if the student is dependent or has a partner. For further information, see:

  • Completing the Claim for ABSTUDY (SY019)
  • Assessing a claim for ABSTUDY Schooling B Award, Tertiary Award and Masters and Doctorate Award
  • Completing the ABSTUDY Additional Information Form (SY023)
  • Assessing the ABSTUDY Additional Information Form (SY023)

6

Students in Lawful Custody Award + Read more ...

Age

No minimum age requirements.

Type of study

  • secondary
  • post-secondary
  • tertiary

Benefits payable

  • Away From Base assistance
  • Lawful Custody Allowance

Eligibility requirements

ABSTUDY eligibility criteria for Lawful Custody Award

Lodgement Requirements when transferring from Part-time Award

An ABSTUDY customer in Lawful Custody (SY022) is not required but is acceptable. If the ABSTUDY customer in Lawful Custody (SY022) is not lodged we do require contact details, location and postal address of the Corrective Institution, plus permission for the Corrective Institution to enquire about the recipient's ABSTUDY. For further information, see:

  • Completing the Claim for ABSTUDY Student or Australian Apprentice in lawful custody (SY022)
  • Assessing the Claim for ABSTUDY Student or Australian Apprentice in lawful custody (SY022)
