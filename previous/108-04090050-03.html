Step

Action

Has the customer requested a revaluation of their market linked managed investments, shares and securities? + Read more ...

  • Yes, go to Step 1 in Table 2

2

20 March and 20 September MFU activities + Read more ...

For unlisted managed investments, see Managed investments - updating existing investments, Process Direct, Step 9 in Initial contact and coding table. Procedure ends here.

For unlisted shares and securities, go to Step 3.

3

Unlisted shares and securities MFU work item + Read more ...

MFU activities for unlisted shares and securities show as an Unlisted share review - General work item with keyword PNSIUSRV. Work item notes recorded on the Shares Summary Unlisted Securities (SIUS) screen, will confirm the company name.

  • Use Super Key to go to screens
  • Key 'START' and select Income and Assets Update from the task selector
  • Select Investments Summary from the task selector options
  • Select Start to initiate the Change of Circumstance flow
  • The Investments Summary (SIS) screen will display
  • Select the relevant unlisted shares and securities on SIS to go to SIUS. For:
    • credit union and/or building society member shares, go to Step 4
    • unlisted share and securities, go to Step 5
    • private company shares, go to Step 8

Note: do not record private company shares on SIUS. These are assessed on the company record by a Complex Assessment Officer (CAO). To check if a company is public or private, search ABN Lookup or ASIC connect.

4

Credit union and/or building society shares recorded on SIUS + Read more ...

Credit union and/or building society shares recorded on SIUS should be transferred to Savings and Direct Investments (SVDI).

  • To update SIUS:
    • select to expand share details
    • on the Shares summary Unlisted Securities Details line, select to open the Create Shares Summary Listed Securities Details
    • update the Number of Shares field to zero (do not delete)
    • check the Asset Test deduction amount field. If it has an entry, update it to zero
    • code Event date (DOV): (the date the MFU activity is being completed)
    • select Save and repeat until all updates are completed

Note: do not use the 'delete' function unless the customer and/or partner never owned the shares listed on their record.

  • To update SVDI:
    • see Coding income and assets for Centrelink payments and services in Process, Table 1, Step 1
    • code Event date (the date the MFU activity is being completed)
  • If there are other unlisted shares and securities, go to Step 5
  • To finalise the MFU review activity, go to Step 10

5

Assessing unlisted shares and securities + Read more ...

Some shares are temporarily coded on SIUS until the ASX code is added to the Shares Summary Listed Securities screen (SILS).

Is the ASX code available on the Select Product (SIP) screen?

  • Yes,
    • zero the 'Total Asset Value' on the SIUS screen, see Updating shares and securities, step 8 in Coding shares and securities in Process Direct table
    • Event date is the date the MFU review activity is being completed
    • add the investment product to SILS, see Adding shares and securities Step 3 in Keying new share or listed securities table
    • go to Step 10 to finalise the MFU review activity
  • No, go to Step 6

6

Obtain current value of unlisted shares + Read more ...

Check the FINS Bulletin for the current share value.

Is the unlisted share value available on the FINS Bulletin

  • Yes, go to Step 7
  • No, see Adding shares and securities, Step 9 (More information needed for unlisted Australian public company shares) and follow the instructions. Procedure ends here

7

Update unlisted share value on SIUS screen + Read more ...

Follow the FINS Bulletin instructions or FINS Helpdesk advice to update the unlisted share value on SIUS screen, see Updating shares and securities, Customer First table, Step 8 (Coding shares and securities).

Event date is the date the MFU review activity is being completed.

Note: multiply the number of shares owned by the new share price to determine the Total Asset Value.

If the number of shares owned is not recorded in the Product field, check prior DOCs and scans for this information.

If these details are not available on the customer's record:

  • contact the customer by phone and confirm the number of shares owned, and
  • add the information to the Product field for future reviews

Go to Step 10 to finalise the MFU review activity.

8

Private company shares recorded on SIUS + Read more ...

Check the Link Summary (LS) screen to see if the customer is linked to the private company.

If the customer is:

  • already linked to the private company record, go to Step 9
  • not linked to the private company, check earlier DOCs and scanned images on the customer's record. Has a MOD PC for the private company been provided:
  • Yes, go to Step 9
  • No, make 2 genuine attempts to contact the customer to discuss the issue. Send a Request for Information for a completed MOD PC and create a review giving the customer time to respond.
    Once the MOD PC is provided, go to Step 9

9

CAO referral + Read more ...

Make a CAO referral and include notes to advise:

  • company shares are incorrectly coded on SIUS, and
  • CAO assessment and SIUS correction are required

Finalise the MFU review.

Add to Notes 'private company shares incorrectly coded on SIUS - referred to CAO'.

Procedure ends here.

10

Finalising updates + Read more ...

Once all updates are completed:

  • select Assess
  • check the payment outcome on Entitlements (ELD)
  • select Finish
  • if the process Direct Income and Assets workflow was used, Notes will pre-fill and display for review
  • add any extra details as per Online Document Recording (ODR)
  • select Finalise

The transaction will complete and Notes will copy to the Document List (DL).

Procedure ends here.

Customer request for revaluation + Read more ...

If the automatic bulk update for 20 March or 20 September has been loaded onto the customer's record, it shows an Event Date in the future.

For example:

  • today's date is 15 March, and
  • the event date for the investment shows 20 March

In these cases, the workaround for MIS/SIS updates immediately prior to 20 March or 20 September must be followed.

If the customer is:

  • asking for a revaluation only, go to Step 2
  • advising details have changed, for example some units or shares have been sold, see:
    • Updating shares and securities, or
    • Managed investments - updating existing investments

2

Bulk update of all listed shares + Read more ...

Tell the customer there is a bulk update of the value of all listed shares and unitised managed investments each March and September.

Does the customer want a revaluation of their shares now?

  • Yes, go to Step 3
  • No, procedure ends here

3

Change in value of one investment + Read more ...

Tell the customer a revaluation will include all their listed shares and unitised managed investments. That may mean the asset value of their total investment portfolio increases.

Offer the customer a trial assessment of their managed investments and/or shares.

Does the customer want a trial assessment?

  • Yes, go to Step 4
  • No, to update:
    • One or more managed investments, see Managed investments - updating existing investments
    • One or more shares, see Updating shares and securities

Procedure ends here.

4

Trial Assessment + Read more ...

A trial assessment is only available in Customer First or Customer Record. Go to Step 4, Table 2 in the Customer First tab for instructions.

Procedure ends here.

Unlisted shares and securities MFU work item + Read more ...

MFU activities for unlisted shares and securities are listed as an Unlisted share review-General work item with keyword PNSIUSRV. Work item notes will confirm the company name, as recorded on the Shares Summary Unlisted Securities (SIUS).

4

Credit union and/or building society shares recorded on SIUS + Read more ...

Credit union and/or building society shares recorded on SIUS should be transferred to Savings and Direct Investments (SVDI).

  • To update SIUS:
    • Total Asset Value: update to zero (do not delete)
    • Annual income: update to zero (if any income keyed)
    • Event Date: is the date the MFU review activity is being completed

Note: do not use the delete function unless the customer and/or partner never owned the shares listed on their record.

  • To update SVDI, see Coding income and assets for Centrelink payments and services, Step 1 in Coding the details of savings accounts and insurance policies table
    • Event Date is the date of the update for the MFU review activity

If there are other unlisted shares and securities, go to Step 5 otherwise finalise the MFU review activity.

Procedure ends here.

    • Zero Total Asset Value on SIUS screen, see Updating shares and securities Step 8 in Coding shares and securities in Customer First table

Note: multiply the number of shares owned by the new share price to determine the Total Asset Value.

If the number of shares owned is not recorded in the Product field, check prior DOCs and scans for this information:

  • If these details are not available on the customer's record, contact the customer by phone and confirm the number of shares owned
  • Once this information is available, add this to the Product field for future reviews

Finalise the MFU review activity.

Private company shares recorded on SIUS + Read more ...

    • If a MOD PC for the private company has been provided, go to Step 9
    • If a MOD PC has not been provided, make 2 genuine attempts to contact the customer to explain the issue
    • Send a request for information to the customer for a completed MOD PC and place review on hold for the allowed time to respond. Once the MOD PC is provided, go to Step 9

4

Trial assessment + Read more ...

In Customer First or Customer Record go to either the:

Managed Investments Summary (MIS) or Shares and Investments Summary (SIS) screens

Make a note of the value of the managed investments or shares. Enter:

  • 'Y' in the Calculate: field
  • the date of event in the Event Date: field
  • 'Y' in the Trial field
  • the appropriate code in the Source: field
  • the date of receipt in the DOR: field
  • Select Continue

Compare the updated values to the previous values. Tell the customer the outcome.

Does the customer want a revaluation of their shares and managed investments?

  • Yes, go to Step 5
  • No:
    • Go to the Activity List (AL) screen
    • delete (D) the trial activity
    • select Continue
    • confirm cancel activity
    • go to Step 8

5

Cancel the trial activity + Read more ...

On the MIS/SIS screens, enter 'N' in the Trial: field.

This converts the trial activity into a normal activity. Finalise the activity on the Assessment Results (AR) screen.

Complete the recalculation.

6

Recalculation + Read more ...

Go to either the MIS or SIS screens in the customer's record.

  • Key 'Y' in the Calculation field
  • Complete the Source and DOR fields
  • Select Continue
  • Complete the activity on the AR screen

7

Recalculation of asset value + Read more ...

The system recalculates the asset value on all the customer's listed shares and unitised managed funds. The current values from the Managed Investments (MIN) and Securities and Investments (SIN) databases are used. The deemed income also updates.

8

Final step + Read more ...

Record details on a DOC.
