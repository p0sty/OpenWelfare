Seed to seed change of organisation ownership (simple change)

Table 1

Role

Action

Responsible Officer (RO) of the new organisation, where

RO of previous organisation continues to act in the same role for the new organisation

Apply online using the Healthcare Identifiers – Change Organisation Ownership application process in HPOS. This will include providing relevant supporting documentation, including:

  • evidence of business identity, such as an Australian Securities and Investments Commission (ASIC) document, for the new owner
  • letter as evidence of the RO’s authority to act on behalf of the seed organisation/ownership with the HI Service

RO of the new organisation, where

organisation has a new RO and/or Organisation Maintenance Officer (OMO)

Apply online using the Healthcare Identifiers – Change Organisation Ownership and Healthcare Identifiers – Manage existing Records application processes in HPOS. This will include providing relevant supporting documentation, including:

  • evidence of business identity, such as an ASIC document, for the new owner

Service Officer

Where an applicant uses the online Healthcare Identifiers – Change Organisation Ownership form in HPOS:

  • Follow the Process online registration for change of ownership – seed organisation

Where an applicant uses the online Healthcare Identifiers – Manage existing Records form in HPOS:

  • Follow the Process online registration for a change of Responsible Officer for seed organisations

Where a manual Application to amend a Healthcare Organisation record form (HW037) or an Application to replace a Responsible Officer or add/remove an Organisation Maintenance Officer for an organisation form (HW040) is provided, note the following:

  • If the organisation name that is changing has been previously verified via the Evidence of Identity (EOI) and authority to act documentation, updated business EOI and authority to act is required
  • end-date details of the previous owners (for example, the organisation name)
  • add the new details with a new start date (date application processed unless future date provided on application)
  • notify the applicant when processing is complete

Note: ABN details can be overwritten and not end-dated.

Seed becomes a network of another seed or network moves to a different hierarchy (complex change)

Table 2: This table describes the required actions of the involved parties when a seed is bought out by another organisation and it, plus some or all of its hierarchy, is transferred into the other organisation's hierarchy, or when a network of one hierarchy is bought by an organisation under a different hierarchy.

Role

Action

Gaining seed organisation Responsible Officer (RO)

Apply online using the Healthcare Identifiers – Change Organisation Ownership application process in HPOS. This will include providing relevant supporting documentation, including:

  • evidence to support the new ownership arrangements
  • a letter stating:
    • the gaining RO has the authority to act on behalf of the incoming organisation
    • the name and Healthcare Provider Identifier - Organisation (HPI-O) of where the incoming organisation is moving to
    • the names and HPI-Os of any networks of the incoming organisation, and how the hierarchy should be structured

Where there is a new Organisation Maintenance Officer (OMO) for the incoming organisation, either:

  • the gaining seed RO or OMO
  • an OMO higher in the hierarchy to the incoming organisation

Apply online using the Healthcare Identifiers – Manage existing Records application process in HPOS.

Previous RO/OMO of incoming organisation

If possible, provide a letter agreeing to the move of the incoming organisation and networks under the gaining seed organisation.

Service Officer

Where an applicant uses the online Healthcare Identifiers – Change Organisation Ownership form in HPOS:

  • Follow the Process online registration for change of ownership – network organisation

Where an applicant uses the online Healthcare Identifiers – Manage existing Records form in HPOS:

  • Follow the Process online registration for a change of Responsible Officer for seed organisations

Where a manual Application to amend a Healthcare Organisation record form (HW037) or an Application to replace a Responsible Officer or add/remove an Organisation Maintenance Officer for an organisation form (HW040) is provided:

  • Ensure all necessary documentation has been provided, and follow up with the applicant if it hasn’t. Note: if any of the networks are not transferring with the organisation, contact the Provider HI Service and My Health Record Subject Matter Expert (SME) for advice before processing
  • Follow the Restructure Healthcare Provider Identifier - Organisation (HPI-O) in Healthcare Identifiers (HI) Service to transfer the incoming organisation to the gaining seed organisation, and to update any network hierarchy changes once the incoming organisation has been transferred
  • Notify the applicant when processing is complete, and advise that the OMO can make future change to the network hierarchy and other details through HPOS

Network becomes a seed organisation (complex change)

Table 3: This table describes the required actions of the new seed organisation, losing seed organisation and HI Service Officer when a network is bought out by a company and it becomes a seed in its own right.

Role

Action

New seed organisation

Apply online using the Healthcare Identifiers – Register Seed Organisation application process in HPOS. This will include providing relevant supporting documentation, including:

  • a letter stating:
    • the names and Healthcare Provider Identifier-Organisations (HPI-O) of any networks that may move with the new seed
    • any changes in the hierarchy structure

Losing seed organisation

Provide a letter agreeing to the loss of the networks moving to the new seed organisation.

Service Officer

Where an applicant uses the online Healthcare Identifiers - Register Seed Organisation form in HPOS:

  • Follow the Process online registrations of HPI-O records for seed organisations

Where a manual Application to register a Seed Organisation form (HW018) is provided:

  • Ensure all necessary documentation has been provided and follow up with the applicant if it hasn’t. Note: if any of the networks are not transferring with the organisation, contact the Provider HI Service and My Health Record Subject Matter Expert (SME) for advice before processing
  • Follow the Restructure Healthcare Provider Identifier - Organisation (HPI-O) in Healthcare Identifiers (HI) Service to transfer the incoming organisation to the gaining seed organisation, and to update any network hierarchy changes once the incoming organisation has been transferred
  • Notify the applicant when processing is complete, and advise that the OMO can make future change to the network hierarchy and other details through HPOS

Issue codes and actions to escalate to the Provider HI Service and My Health Record SME

Table 4

Item

Action

1

Organisation name may be invalid or truncated

Check documents to ensure organisation name is correct.

If name is:

  • truncated:
    • override, select the Override checkbox
    • copy and paste the text ‘Organisation name checked. Correct or appropriately truncated’ into the Comments box
    • for a seed organisation, go to Step 2 of Table 2
    • for a network organisation, go to Step 2 of Table 3
  • invalid, escalate to the Provider HI Service and My Health Record Subject Matter Expert (SME)

2

Other issues

If the below errors occur, escalate the application ID to the Provider HI Service and My Health Record SME via email and re-categorise the work item in WLM to Healthcare Identifier Tier 3 Escalation.

  • ABN is considered restricted
  • ACN is considered restricted
  • Organisation type is considered restricted
