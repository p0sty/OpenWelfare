Check source of encounter (IM018) and history (IM013) forms

Table 1

Step

Action

1

Check source location on PaNDA + Read more ...

Check the Source Location for the form on the PaNDA work item.

If received via:

  • HPOS, go to Step 2
  • Email or SOC (Scanning Operations Centre), go to Step 5

2

Check source of form received via HPOS + Read more ...

In the PaNDA work item:

  • Select Open in HPOS in top right corner of work item
  • Check Sent for details in Text to see who sent the form:
    • Doctor - doctor's name shows
    • Delegate - shows as Sent for doctor's name, by delegate's name
    • Organisation - shows as organisation's name

If the form was sent by:

  • doctor, delegate or organisation, see Table 2
  • unidentified, go to Step 3

3

HPOS form sent by unidentified source + Read more ...

Try once to contact the practice to find out who sent the form.

For outbound calls, Service Officers must use the script in Step 5 in the Authentication of recognised vaccination providers for AIR enquiries table.

If the practice confirms they sent the form:

  • tell them to transmit via doctor/delegate/organisation in future
  • add comments in PaNDA and mainframe - Confirmed with (doctor/nurse/receptionist) that a staff member sent form via HPOS
  • go to Table 2

If the practice did not send the form or they cannot be contacted, go to Step 4.

4

HPOS form sent by patient, other or unable to contact practice + Read more ...

  • Send the form back to the patient with Z2334 standard letter. Do not action
  • Add comments in PaNDA and mainframe - Form submitted by (patient/unknown person), returned with letter'
  • Check the correct Medicare number and provider number have been saved on the work item
  • Complete the work item in PaNDA
  • Procedure ends here

5

Form received via email or SOC + Read more ...

Try once to contact the practice.

For outbound calls, Service Officers must use the script in Step 5 in the Authentication of recognised vaccination providers for AIR enquiries table.

If contact is:

  • successful, go to Step 6
  • unsuccessful, go to Step 8

6

Confirm practice sent the form + Read more ...

Tell the practice contact that when forms are received manually, Services Australia will contact them to confirm they sent the form.

Promote digital options

If the promotion of digital options generates a negative response from the practice, email Air Support and request a Business Development Officer (BDO) referral.

Include in email:

  • Subject heading - Manual Lodgement, Provider Number
  • Attach a copy of the form that prompted the call
  • Details of:
    • who the conversation was with
    • the initial conversation/outcome
    • the reason for not submitting digitally

If the Service officer is:

  • internet trained, help the practice contact to organise another time to call back
  • not internet trained, offer to transfer the practice contact to the AIR Internet helpdesk and give the practice the phone number

Did the practice contact confirm that they sent the form?

  • Yes, go to Step 7
  • No, go to Step 8

7

Confirm provider signed the form + Read more ...

Has the provider signed the form?

  • Yes:
    • Confirm it was sent manually
    • Add comments in PaNDA and mainframe - Confirmed with (doctor/nurse/receptionist) form sent by practice'
    • Process the form, see Table 2
  • No:
    • Confirm it was completed by a provider and sent manually
    • Add comments in PaNDA and mainframe - Spoke to (doctor/nurse/receptionist) and confirmed form completed and sent by practice
    • Process the form, see Table 2

Note: if the form is received via email and it is clear the practice sent it, see Table 2

8

Unable to contact practice or confirm sender + Read more ...

If the Service Officer is unable to determine who sent the form:

  • return the form to the patient with Z2334 standard letter
  • add comment in PaNDA and mainframe - Unable to confirm who sent the form, returned with letter
  • check the correct Medicare number and provider number have been saved on the work item
  • complete work item in PaNDA. See Work Optimiser for staff
  • procedure ends here

3

Create immunisation encounter header + Read more ...

In AIR mainframe:

  • key YIAM
  • the YIAM - Create/Amend Header Register Manual Claim screen displays
  • key the Information Provider Number, Claim ID, Quoted Number and Receipt Date (date form signed)
  • press [Enter]
  • the message PF9 to update displays
  • press [PF9 - UPDATE]. The YXCB - Individual Selection screen displays
  • search for the individual
  • press [Enter]
  • the YIAE - Encounter Details screen displays
  • press [Enter]. The message 'PF9 to update' displays
  • press [PF9 - UPDATE]
  • the YIAN - Antigen Details screen displays
  • key the vaccine code in the vaccine code field
  • key dose number in the dose field
  • key the batch number
    • Astra Zeneca and Pfizer vaccines have a lot number instead of a batch number. Enter the lot number
  • press [Enter] and [PF9] to update

If batch number is not recorded for National Immunisation Program (NIP), COVID-19 or Influenza vaccines:

  • Service Officer must attempt one outbound call to the provider
  • If contact is unsuccessful, send Z2334 standard letter

For multiple encounters, go to Step 4

If any of the antigens for COVID-19 were administered overseas and the country of administration is listed on the form:

  • Service Officer must complete the LPS referral form
  • LPS will add the country of administration

Note:

  • If error message is returned, see the Header error messages table
  • If amendments to header details are required, see Table 6
  • To obtain a provider number where a header form has not been supplied with an encounter form batch, see Table 7

4

Multiple encounters + Read more ...

To locate individual:

  • press [PF15]
  • the YXCB - Individual Selection screen will display
  • key Medicare card number (if available), or
  • personal details such as:
    • surname
    • given name
    • gender
    • date of birth
    • postcode (optional)
  • press [Enter]
  • the YIAE - Encounter Details screen displays when a unique match or list of possible matches is displayed on the YXCB - Selection screen
  • select the match from the list to display the YIAE - Encounter Details screen

If any of the antigens for COVID-19 were administered overseas and the country of administration is listed on the form:

  • Service Officer must complete the LPS referral form
  • LPS will add the country of administration

5

Determine if the matching individual is located + Read more ...

Is the individual's record located?

  • Yes, go to Step 7
  • No, go to Step 6
    • The message Amend search details or PF10 to add child is displayed, or
    • List of possible matches found, scroll list to check for a match
    • Search Medicare CDMS for the individual. If the individual cannot be found then the individual may be added to the AIR creating a SIN (Supplementary Identification Number) record

6

Add new individual + Read more ...

To add the individual:

  • from the YXCB - Selection screen, remove Medicare number if present
  • press [PF10] - new child or individual to the AIR as a SIN record

Key either a Medicare address or an address supplied by the provider when adding an individual. It is also important to key the individual's gender.

7

Update and record vaccines + Read more ...

To record vaccination details:

  • enter date the immunisation was given in the Imm Date field
  • press [PF9] to update
  • key vaccine code and dose in applicable fields on the YIAN - Antigen Details screen
  • press [Enter]
  • check that there are no pends by looking for an A in the ST field and 00 in the Pend Count field. If there are pends, action pended antigen with appropriate action code
  • if all vaccines have an A status, press [PF9 - UPDATE]
  • the YIAE - Encounter Details screen displays
  • finalise the document as per processing guidelines

Note: if recording a hepatitis B birth dose from the immunisation encounter form, enter B in the Dose field if given to a child up to 7 days of age.

See AIR system for a list of vaccine code names or place cursor on VACC field and select [PF1-HELP] to display a list of vaccine codes.

8

Update indigenous details + Read more ...

Check the Medicare CDMS personal tab

Are the details listed on Medicare CDMS?

  • Yes, do not update the record
  • No,
    • key YCUI anywhere on a blank screen
    • the YCUI - Registration Amendment Search screen displays
    • key Medicare number or individual's details
    • press [Enter]
    • the YCUA - Amendment Registration screen displays
    • key Y in the Indigenous field
    • press [Enter]
    • the message PF9 to update displays
    • press [PF9 - UPDATE]
    • the message update successful will display.
    • procedure ends here

Abandon/delete a claim for the AIR

Table 8: only abandon a claim if:

  • a payment to the provider has not been made (for encounter claims only)
  • a claim has been recorded and the incorrect provider information has been keyed or the header information has been keyed twice. Note: identifying a claim as being keyed twice, the claim sequence number is 002

    Step

    Action

    1

    Access Immunisation Encounters menu + Read more ...

    To access the Immunisation Encounters menu:

    • key YIEM
    • press [Enter]

    2

    Access processing screen + Read more ...

    To access Abandon Claim screen:

    • press [PF6] - Abandon claim
    • press [Enter]
    • the YICA - Abandon Claim Processing screen displays

    3

    Locate list of claims + Read more ...

    To locate claim to abandon:

    • key provider number
    • key Claim ID (an asterisk * is required after claim ID if abandoning a history form claim)
    • press [Enter]

    4

    Select claim(s) to abandon + Read more ...

    To select claim to abandon:

    • select (/) claim to be deleted

    More than one claim may be abandoned at the same time.

    Action ODDs work item

    Table 10: re-categorise in PaNDA immunisation information when an approved form is not available as 'AIR enquiry' work type. Service Officers with the appropriate skill tag will action this. For example, requests from the public, removal of ceasing correspondence instruction.

    Step

    Action

    1

    Assess document + Read more ...

    Use the AIR work types quick reference guide on the Resources page to determine if the data can be lodged online:

    • Yes, go to Step 2
    • No,
      • If the data can be collected on a form, go to Step 3
      • If there is no other means of collecting this information, process the document and go to Step 3

    2

    Access to online lodgement channel + Read more ...

    Determine if the provider or their associated practice currently has access to an online lodgement channel:

    • Perform a provider number search on the AIR secure website (search by both individual or practice provider number, if available)
    • View the Claim history screens in AIR mainframe and see if provider has previously lodged a claim using online channels (see Identifying data lodgement methods on the Resources page)

    Does the provider or their associated practice have access to an online lodgement channel?

    • Yes, AIR site access or previous online channel lodgement activity evident, go to Step 3 (provider does have online capability)
    • No, no previous online activity evident, go to Step 3 (Provider does not have online capability but data can be lodged on an AIR form)

    3

    Information lodged based on provider capability + Read more ...

    Check comments on the providers AIR Program Registration PDS record about previous (ODDs).

    If the Provider:

    • has online capability:
      • Return the paperwork using the We need more information to process your form Z2334 letter
      • go to Step 4
    • Has online capability and information already recorded:
      • do not update
      • do not return with letter
      • go to Step 5
    • does not have online capability but data can be lodged on an AIR form:
      • return the paperwork using the We need more information to process your form Z2334 letter
      • go to Step 4

    If a work item cannot be lodged through the AIR website, Practice Management Software or an authorised form, go to Step 4 (Refer to a LPS for forwarding to an AIR BDO to contact provider)

    4

    Recording a standard comment in the Provider Directory System (PDS) + Read more ...

    Service Officers processing ODDs work types must record a comment on the providers AIR Program Registration page in PDS to alert other Service Officers. Refer ODDS work items to Local Peer Support if the provider has continuing multiple notes on record.

    Referral to a LPS

    If a provider has continuing multiple notes for sending ODDs, or more intensive assistance is required:

    • complete the LPS Referral form
    • attach the ODDs documentation, and
    • escalate to LPS with a brief explanation of steps taken

    The LPS will make a determination to:

    • process the data (re-allocate work back to Service Officer), or
    • make a referral using the HPSE (AIR BDO) Referral form, if the assistance of a Business Development Officer is required

    The LPS will record a comment on the provider's AIR Program Registration page in PDS to alert staff to the escalation.

    Referral to an AIR Business Development Officer (AIR BDO)

    The AIR BDO will contact the provider to assist them access online lodgement methods. If required, the AIR BDO will record outcome of their engagement on the program registration Comments section of the PDS record.

    5

    Finalise processing + Read more ...

    Finalise the document as per processing guidelines.
