This page contains resources relevant to WA Ex-nuptial cases. When providing advice to customers or processing work, consideration must be given to whether there are implications for WA Ex-nuptial cases. Specific procedures have been included in all WA Ex-nuptial impacted products.

Blueprint documents updates

Table 1: This table contains guidance on providing advice and managing work for WA Ex-nuptial cases on or after 15 May 2019.

Item

Description

1

Care change – child support agreement + Read more ...

If the care a payee has of a child of an agreement falls below 35%, the agreement may be:

  • suspended, or
  • terminated

If notified of a care change for a child where child support is paid under an agreement, the care is processed as normal.

WA Ex-nuptial cases with such care changes are identified by reports. They are resolved and resolved the Court Order/Agreement Team.

2

Amended tax assessments + Read more ...

From 23 May 2018, 15 May 2019 for WA Ex-nuptials, if the ATO amends a customer’s tax assessment, Child Support can generally use the amended income to change their child support assessment.

Whether the amended tax assessment will be used retrospectively or prospectively depends on various factors.

See Australian Tax Office (ATO) income for Child Support customers.

A system enhancement is required to automatically give effect to this. Until Cuba is updated, manual workarounds ensure the child support assessment is correct.

WA Ex-nuptial cases with amended tax assessments are identified by reports. They are resolved by specialised teams.

3

Child Support Estimates + Read more ...

Reconcile

When reconciling an estimate for WA Ex-nuptial customers, determine the reconciling amount over the entire estimate period. The legislation and the income components to apply to the reconciling amount will depend on whether the first estimate of the child support period was lodged before or after 3 March 2011.

See Child support estimates – Reconcile (pre 1 July 2011).

Review (Post 1 July 2010)

When reviewing an estimate for WA Ex-nuptial customers, determine the income to use in the review. This is the amount of taxable income plus other component amounts. Post 1 July 2010 legislation will apply to the estimate review if the first estimate of the child support period was lodged on or after 3 March 2011.

See Child support estimates review (post 1 July 2010).

Review (Pre 1 July 2010)

Pre 1 July 2010 legislation will apply to WA Ex-nuptial estimate reviews if the first estimate of the child support period was lodged before 3 March 2011. The estimate income components will comprise of:

  • taxable income
  • reportable fringe benefits
  • target foreign income
  • total net rental property losses, and
  • tax free pensions and benefits

See Child support estimates review (pre 1 July 2010).

4

Minimum annual rate to nil – Reducing child support assessments + Read more ...

WA Ex-nuptial customers may only nominate a period of less than 12 months if the application date and the nominated period begins no earlier than 3 March 2011.

See Minimum annual rate to nil – Reducing child support assessments.

Cuba process and intray helps updates

Table 2

Item

Description

1

Income Cuba Process Help + Read more ...

If the first estimate of the child support period was lodged before 3 March 2011, when processing subsequent estimate elections (in the same child support period):

  • A notified rental loss by the customer is to be recorded as a negative value in the Net Rental Income field – this will then auto-populate the correct amount in the Total Net Investment Losses field
  • Positive rental income will reflect accordingly in the taxable income and is not a supplementary amount
  • Reportable superannuation contributions and net financial income are not required, the Reportable Superannuation field and Net Financial Investment Income field will not be able to be entered

When reconciling an estimate, the Net Financial Investment Income, Financial Investment Loss and Reportable Superannuation fields will be disabled for WA Ex-nuptial cases if the first estimate of the child support period was lodged before 3 March 2011.

See Income Cuba Process Help.

2

Care - WA Ex-nuptial intray help + Read more ...

Where a Centrelink care change is received for care period before 3 March 2011, process in line with pre 1 July 2010 legislation as Alignment of Care legislation was adopted in Western Australia on 3 March 2011.

See Care intrays Cuba Process Help – Care – WA Ex-nuptial intray Help.
