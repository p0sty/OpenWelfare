Frequently asked questions

For FAQs relevant to all Complex programs, see Processing Complex Authority Required Listings.

Item

Description

1

Question: Does the first continuing have to be after 12 weeks?

Answer: Yes, the patient must have at least 12 weeks of therapy to demonstrate a response to treatment. Measure 12 weeks from approval date.

2

Question: The prescriber provided both ESR and CRP at baseline but is only responding on CRP currently – is this ok?

Answer: Yes. As long as both bloods were qualifying for entry at baseline, only responding on one of them currently is ok.
