IM and enhanced IM customers who HAVE a payment nominee

Table 1: This table outlines the arrangements for those customers who have a payment nominee under the various IM and enhanced IM measures.

Item

Details

1

Cape York + Read more ...

Under Cape York, there are two possible outcomes for customers who have a payment nominee:

  • If the payment nominee is an excluded payment nominee for Income Management or enhanced Income Management purposes, the customer is not eligible for IM or enhanced IM. Income Management Contact Officers (IMCO) are responsible for ineligibility coding - see Nominee Income Management and enhanced Income Management. It is at the discretion of the Family Responsibilities Commission if they choose to revoke the current Notice
  • If the payment nominee is not an excluded payment nominee, and the customer meets all other eligibility criteria, the customer will be income managed, with the payment nominee being responsible for managing the enhanced IM account of the principal

A review of the payment nominee arrangement must be completed for customers who have been referred to enhanced IM under Cape York and have a payment nominee in place, regardless of whether the nominee is an excluded payment nominee.

Note: termination of a payment nominee arrangement may result in a customer again becoming eligible for enhanced IM under Cape York, where that payment nominee was an excluded payment nominee.

2

Child Protection IM (CPIM) and enhanced IM (CPeIM) + Read more ...

Under CPIM and CPeIM, there are two possible outcomes for customers who have a payment nominee:

  • If the payment nominee is an excluded payment nominee for Income Management or enhanced Income Management purposes, the customer is not eligible for IM or enhanced IM. Income Management Contact Officers (IMCO) are responsible for ineligibility coding - see Nominee Income Management and enhanced Income Management. It is at the discretion of the Child Protection Authority if they choose to revoke the current Notice
  • If the payment nominee is not an excluded payment nominee and the customer meets all other eligibility criteria, the customer will be welfare quarantined with the payment nominee being responsible for determining allocations from the IM account or managing the enhanced IM account of the principal

A review of the payment nominee arrangement must be completed for customers who have been referred to CPIM or CPeIM and have a payment nominee in place, regardless of whether the nominee is an excluded payment nominee.

Note: termination of a payment nominee arrangement may result in a customer again becoming eligible for CPeIM where that payment nominee was an excluded payment nominee.

3

Supporting People at Risk + Read more ...

Under the Supporting People at Risk (SPaR) measure, there are two possible outcomes for customers who have a payment nominee:

  • If the payment nominee is an excluded payment nominee for Income Management or enhanced Income Management purposes, the customer is not eligible for IM or enhanced IM. Income Management Contact Officers (IMCO) are responsible for ineligibility coding - see Nominee Income Management and enhanced Income Management. It is at the discretion of the approved referring authority if they choose to revoke the current Notice
  • If the payment nominee is not an excluded payment nominee and the customer meets all other eligibility criteria, the customer will be welfare quarantined with the payment nominee being responsible for determining allocations from the IM account or managing the enhanced IM account of the principal

A review of the payment nominee arrangement must be completed for customers who have been referred to IM or enhanced IM under the SPaR measure and have a payment nominee in place, regardless of whether the nominee is an excluded payment nominee.

Note: termination of a payment nominee arrangement may result in a customer again becoming eligible for enhanced IM under the SPaR measure where that payment nominee was an excluded payment nominee.

4

Vulnerable Welfare Payment Recipient (VWPR) automatic triggers + Read more ...

Under the Vulnerable Welfare Payment Recipient (VWPR) measure, there are two possible outcomes for customers who have a payment nominee:

  • If the payment nominee is an excluded payment nominee for Income Management or enhanced Income Management purposes, the customer is not eligible for IM or enhanced IM. For more information, see Nominee Income Management and enhanced Income Management.
  • If the payment nominee is not an excluded payment nominee, and the customer meets all other eligible criteria, the customer will be welfare quarantined, with the payment nominee being responsible for determining allocations from the IM account or managing the enhanced IM account of the principal

A review of the payment nominee arrangement must be completed for customers who have been referred to enhanced IM under the VWPR measure by a social worker and have a payment nominee in place, regardless of whether the nominee is an excluded payment nominee.

Note: termination of a payment nominee arrangement may result in a customer again becoming eligible for enhanced IM under the VWPR measure where that payment nominee was an excluded payment nominee.

5

Disengaged Youth (DEY) + Read more ...

Under the Disengaged Youth (DEY) measure, there are three possible outcomes for customers who have a payment nominee:

  • If the payment nominee is an excluded payment nominee for Income Management or enhanced Income Management purposes, the customer is not eligible for IM or enhanced IM For more information, see Nominee Income Management and enhanced Income Management.
  • If the payment nominee is not an excluded payment nominee, and the customer meets all other eligibility criteria, the customer will be welfare quarantined with the payment nominee being responsible for determining allocations from the IM account or managing the enhanced IM account of the principal
  • If the payment nominee has an exemption from IM or enhanced IM then this exemption will also apply to the principal if they would have been welfare quarantined under NIM or NeIM

Note:

  • Termination of a payment nominee arrangement may result in a customer again becoming eligible for enhanced IM under the DEY measure where that payment nominee was an excluded payment nominee
  • Customers on NIM or NeIM under the Disengaged Youth measure are not eligible to apply for an exemption

6

Long Term Welfare Payment Recipient (LTWPR) + Read more ...

Under the Long Term Welfare Payment Recipient (LTWPR) measure, there are three possible outcomes for customers who have a payment nominee:

  • If the payment nominee is an excluded payment nominee for Income Management or enhanced Income Management purposes, the customer is not eligible for IM or enhanced IM. For more information, see Nominee Income Management and enhanced Income Management.
  • If the payment nominee is not an excluded payment nominee, and the customer meets all other eligibility criteria, the customer will be welfare quarantined with the payment nominee being responsible for determining allocations from the IM account or managing the enhanced IM account of the principal
  • If the payment nominee has an exemption from IM or enhanced IM then this exemption will also apply to the principal if they would have been welfare quarantined under NIM-CIM or NeIM-CeIM

Note:

  • Termination of a payment nominee arrangement may result in a customer again becoming eligible for enhanced IM under the LTWPR measure where that payment nominee was an excluded payment nominee
  • Customers on NIM or NeIM under the Long Term Welfare Payment Recipient measure are not eligible to apply for an exemption

7

Voluntary Income Management and enhanced IM + Read more ...

A customer who has an excluded payment nominee for Income Management or enhanced Income Management purposes, is not eligible to enter a Voluntary IM (VIM) or enhanced IM (VeIM) Agreement.

For more information about terminating a VIM or VeIM agreement as the customer is no longer eligible, see Nominee Income Management and enhanced Income Management

However, if the payment nominee is welfare quarantined themselves, a VeIM Agreement can be entered into.

Note: customers who are on NIM or NeIM are not eligible to enter a VIM or VeIM Agreement.

A review of the payment nominee arrangement must be completed for customers who request to enter a VIM or VeIM Agreement and have a current payment nominee in place, regardless of whether the nominee is an excluded payment nominee.

8

BasicsCard + Read more ...

A payment nominee can decide to have the principal (customer) issued with the BasicsCard. The system identifies that a customer has a payment nominee and will bring up an assign card screen at the end of the authentication screen. Here the payment nominee can choose to have the BasicsCard issued in the principal's (customers) name rather than their own.

Should the payment nominee decide to have the card assigned to the principal (customer), the principal will need to sign the BasicsCard receipt.

Note: if an under 18 year old Youth Allowance customer is income managed in their own right, and wishes to have the BasicsCard issued in their parents name and the parent approves then the under 18 year old Youth Allowance customer must appoint their parent as a formal payment nominee.

If the Nominee is on enhanced IM they will not be issued BasicsCard for the principal, see SmartCards and enhanced IM account

9

Regular allocations + Read more ...

A payment nominee can control one off and regular allocations on behalf of the customer (principal).

10

SmartCard and enhanced IM account + Read more ...

The card issuer will issue the SmartCard to the payment nominee. The payment nominee’s name is printed on the front of the card (the principal’s name is printed underneath the nominee’s name), and the BSB code and account number on the back of the card. The enhanced IM account that is linked to the SmartCard is also managed by the payment nominee.

In some cases, the payment nominee may request for the SmartCard to be issued in the principal’s name - in these cases, both the principal and the payment nominee will have access to the enhanced IM account.

IM and enhanced IM customers who ARE a payment nominee

Table 2: This table outlines the arrangements for those customers who are a payment nominee under the various IM and enhanced IM measures.

Item

Details

1

Cape York + Read more ...

A customer referred for enhanced IM under Cape York who is a payment nominee for another person must have their payment nominee appointment reconsidered to determine if the arrangement is appropriate and in the best interests of the principal.

If the appointment is deemed appropriate:

  • the customer that is welfare quarantined under Cape York can continue to be a payment nominee
  • the principal will be welfare quarantined under Nominee enhanced IM - Cape York (NeIM-CY), unless they are eligible under another measure in their own right, and
  • the principal's income support payments will continue to be managed by the customer as the payment nominee (that is, the applicable percentage paid to the principal's Income Management account or enhanced IM account and the un-qualified funds paid to the payment nominee)

2

Child Protection IM (CPIM) and enhanced IM (CPeIM) + Read more ...

A customer referred for CPIM and CPeIM must have their payment nominee appointment reconsidered to determine if the arrangement is appropriate and in the best interests of the principal.

If the appointment is deemed appropriate:

  • the customer that is welfare quarantined under CPIM or CPeIM can continue to be a payment nominee
  • the principal will be welfare quarantined under NIM – Child Protection (NIM-CP) or NeIM -Child Protection (NeIM-CP), unless they are eligible under another measure in their own right, and
  • the principal's income support payments will continue to be managed by the customer as the payment nominee (that is, the applicable percentage paid to the principal's Income Management account or enhanced IM account and the un-qualified funds paid to the payment nominee)

Customers who are paid instalments of Youth Allowance (YA) on behalf of a Youth Allowance principal, and are on CPIM or CPeIM, will not have these instalments income managed (unless the Youth Allowance principal is welfare quarantined in their own right).

3

Supporting People at Risk + Read more ...

A customer referred for IM or enhanced IM under the SPaR measure must have their payment nominee appointment reconsidered to determine if the arrangement is appropriate and in the best interests of the principal.

If the appointment is deemed appropriate:

  • the customer that is welfare quarantined under the SPaR measure can continue to be a payment nominee,
  • the principal will be welfare quarantined under NIM - Supporting People at Risk (NIM-SPAR) or NeIM Supporting People at Risk (NeIM-SPAR), unless they are eligible under another measure in their own right, and
  • the principal's income support payments will continue to be managed by the customer as the payment nominee (that is, the applicable percentage paid to the principal's Income Management account or enhanced IM account and the un-quarantined funds paid to the payment nominee)

4

Vulnerable Welfare Payment Recipient (VWPR) notice + Read more ...

A customer referred for IM or enhanced IM under the Vulnerable Welfare Payment Recipient (VWPR) measure by a social worker, must have their payment nominee appointment reconsidered to determine if the arrangement is appropriate and in the best interest of the customer.

Customers identified for IM or enhanced IM under the Vulnerable Welfare Payment Recipient (VWPR) measure, and are a payment nominee, should have the payment nominee arrangement reviewed.

If the appointment is deemed appropriate:

  • the customer that is welfare quarantined under the VWPR measure can continue to be a payment nominee, and
  • the principal's income support payments will continue to be managed by the customer as the payment nominee (that is, the full amount paid directly to the payment nominee where the principal is not on IM or enhanced IM, or if the principal is on IM or enhanced IM, the relevant percentage paid to the Income Management account or enhanced IM account and the un-quarantined funds paid to the payment nominee)

5

Disengaged Youth (DEY) + Read more ...

Customers identified as eligible for IM and enhanced IM under the Disengaged Youth (DEY) measure, and are a payment nominee, should have the payment nominee arrangement reviewed.

If the payment nominee arrangement is to continue:

  • the customer that is welfare quarantined under the DEY measure can continue to be a payment nominee
  • the principal will be welfare quarantined under NIM Compulsory (NIM-CIM) or NeIM – Compulsory (NeIM-CeIM), unless they are eligible under another measure in their own right
  • the principal's payments will be managed by the customer as the payment nominee (that is, 50% paid to the principal's IM account or enhanced IM account and 50% paid directly to the payment nominee, or applicable percentage if the principal is welfare quarantined under another measure in their own right)
  • if the payment nominee has an exemption from IM or enhanced IM then this exemption will also apply to the principal if they would have been welfare quarantined under NIM or NeIM

6

Long Term Welfare Payment Recipient (LTWPR) + Read more ...

Long Term Welfare Payment Customers identified as eligible for IM and enhanced IM under the Long Term Welfare Payment Recipient (LTWPR) measure, and who are a payment nominee, should have the payment nominee arrangement reviewed.

If the payment nominee arrangement is to continue:

  • the customer that is welfare quarantined under the LTWPR measure can continue to be a payment nominee
  • the principal will be welfare quarantined under NIM-CIM or NeIM-CeIM, unless they are eligible under another measure in their own right
  • the principal's payments will be managed by the customer as the payment nominee (that is, 50% paid to the principal's IM account or enhanced IM account and 50% paid directly to the payment nominee, or applicable percentage if the principal is welfare quarantined under another measure in their own right)
  • if the payment nominee has an exemption from IM or enhanced IM then this exemption will also apply to the principal if they would have been welfare quarantined under NIM or NeIM

7

Voluntary Income Management and enhanced IM + Read more ...

There is no restriction for customers who are payment nominees and want to enter into a VIM or VeIM Agreement, however, where the customer was previously an excluded payment nominee for Income Management or enhanced Income Management purposes, the principal may now be eligible for enhanced IM.

A review of the payment nominee arrangement must be completed for customers who are payment nominees and are requesting to enter a VeIM agreement.

If the appointment is deemed appropriate:

  • the customer that is welfare quarantined under VeIM can continue to be a payment nominee, and
  • the principal's income support payments will continue to be managed by the customer as the payment nominee (that is, the full amount paid directly to the payment nominee where the principal is not on IM or enhanced IM, or if the principal is welfare quarantined, the relevant percentage paid to the principal's IM account or enhanced IM account and the discretionary funds paid directly to the payment nominee)

8

BasicsCard + Read more ...

Customers who are payment nominees and on IM Management may hold two BasicsCards, one for themselves and one for the principal for whom they are the nominee (if they have been issued with the principal's BasicsCard).

If the principal is on enhanced IM they will not be issued BasicsCard for the principal, see SmartCards and enhanced IM account.

 

9

SmartCard and enhanced IM account + Read more ...

Customers who are payment nominees and on enhanced IM may hold two SmartCards, one for themselves and one for the principal for whom they are the nominee.

If the principal is on IM, the payment nominee may have a BasicsCard (if they have been issued with the principal’s BasicsCard), see BasicsCard

IM and enhanced IM correspondence nominee arrangements

Table 3: This table outlines the arrangements for those customers who have a correspondence nominee in relation to BasicsCard and Regular allocations.

Item

Customers who have a correspondence nominee

1

BasicsCard + Read more ...

The correspondence nominee cannot be issued with the BasicsCard.

If the customer wants a correspondence nominee to be issued a BasicsCard, they will need to request the appointment of a nominee using online accounts or complete the Authorising a person or organisation to enquire or act on your behalf (SS313) to appoint the correspondence nominee as a payment nominee as well. Expense allocations

If the customer does not have a payment nominee, a correspondence nominee can act as if they were the customer (principal for Income Management and enhanced Income Management purposes) when deciding on expense allocations. In all circumstances the Service Officer should have regard to the best interests of the customer (principal for Income Management purposes) and the customer's dependants.

2

SmartCard + Read more ...

The correspondence nominee cannot be issued with a SmartCard for an enhanced IM customer.

If the customer wants a correspondence nominee to be issued a SmartCard, they will need to complete the Authorising a person or organisation to enquire or act on your behalf (SS313) to appoint the correspondence nominee as a payment nominee as well.

3

Regular allocations IM + Read more ...

Correspondence nominees can act as if they were the customer (principal for Income Management purposes) when deciding on expense allocations.

In all circumstances the Service Officer should have regard to the best interests of the customer (principal for Income Management purposes) and the customer's dependents.

The correspondence nominee cannot be issued with the BasicsCard.

If the customer wants a correspondence nominee to be issued a BasicsCard, the customer will need to request the appointment of a nominee using online accounts or complete the Authorising a person or organisation to enquire or act on your behalf (SS313) to appoint the correspondence nominee as a payment nominee as well.

4

Voluntary IM (VIM) or enhanced IM (VeIM) + Read more ...

If a correspondence nominee requests to enter or exit VIM or VeIM on behalf of a customer (principal), Services Australia must confirm with the principal (customer) is in agreement with the instructions of the correspondence nominee prior to commencing or ending a VIM or VeIM Agreement.
