Eligibility for ABSTUDY Standard Hostels Agreement

Table 1: This table describes the steps in determining if the hostel is a signatory to the ABSTUDY Standard Hostels Agreement and checking eligibility for the payment.

Step

Action

1

ABSTUDY Standard Hostels Agreement + Read more ...

For students residing in a hostel, the ABSTUDY Standard Hostels Agreement determines the conditions for payment of:

  • ABSTUDY away from home rate of Living Allowance
  • Rent Assistance, and
  • Remote Area Allowance

Hostels which are signatories to the Agreement can be paid ABSTUDY as an ABSTUDY boarding instalment. If a:

  • hostel is seeking payment via the ABSTUDY boarding instalment method, go to Step 2
  • review has matured on a hostel organisation record which identifies an ABSTUDY Standard Hostels Agreement has, or is about to expire, go to Step 3

2

Hostel organisation record + Read more ...

Check the Document List (DL) on the hostel's organisation record in Customer First. If a hostel is a signatory to the ABSTUDY Standard Hostels Agreement for the current 3 year period, there should be a DOC noting this.

A hostel that:

  • has an Agreement for a previous expired period, is deemed to not have a valid current Agreement
  • is not covered by the AHL Bilateral Agreement, needs to have an individual agreement for each hostel they operate - even if the hostels are operated by the same over-arching Company

Note: the Resources page lists all hostels who are signatories to the Agreement and each individual expiry date.

Is the individual hostel a signatory to the ABSTUDY Standard Hostels Agreement for the current 3 year period?

  • Yes, see Step 1 in Table 2
  • No, go to Step 3

3

Services Australia and AHL National Bilateral Agreement + Read more ...

A number of Aboriginal Hostels Limited (AHL) hostels provide dedicated student accommodation for secondary school students living away from home. Services Australia and AHL have signed a national Agreement governing the conditions of payment of ABSTUDY away from home allowances. These hostels are not required to sign individual Agreements with Services Australia.

Note: hostels covered by the national Agreement with AHL are listed in the Resources page and are identified on the individual hostel record via a DOC.

Is the hostel covered by the AHL Bilateral Agreement that has expired?

  • Yes, go to Step 6
  • No, go to Step 4

4

ABSTUDY Standard Hostels Agreement + Read more ...

The hostel must enter into an ABSTUDY Standard Hostels Agreement for the current 3 year period in order to receive ABSTUDY away from home allowances paid as an ABSTUDY boarding instalment.

Has the hostel been a signatory to an Agreement previously?

  • Yes, go to Step 6
  • No, go to Step 5

5

New ABSTUDY Standard Hostels Agreement + Read more ...

To enter into an ABSTUDY Standard Hostels Agreement an organisation must first have met:

  • all local and State/Territory government requirements to operate as a hostel. This includes local council approval for 'business use of the premises' as a hostel or lodging house
  • local building and facility requirements
  • State/Territory governments various requirements to operate as an accommodation facility for children

Before entering into an agreement, contact the hostel. Explain the necessity of the hostel having all the necessary government approvals to operate as a hostel. This will ensure the hostel is able to access ABSTUDY away from home allowances via the ABSTUDY boarding instalment payment method.

Note: if a hostel is seeking guidance as to the appropriate approval process and requirements to operate as a hostel, refer them to their local council.

Go to Step 6.

6

Negotiating the ABSTUDY Standard Hostels Agreement + Read more ...

Advise the hostel:

  • that an Agreement must be entered into, or renewed if previously a signatory, in order to access ABSTUDY away from home allowances via the ABSTUDY boarding instalment payment method
  • that Services Australia will make contact with them within 7 days to discuss a new agreement

Note: the ABSTUDY and AIC Programme Team has responsibility of negotiating all ABSTUDY Standard Hostels Agreements and communicating the outcome with the relevant provider.

New agreements - unless an Agreement is entered into:

  • payments will be made for each qualified student via the fortnight-in-arrears payment method, and
  • unused Group 2 School Fees cannot be transferred to top up any board shortfall

Expired agreements - payments for current and new ABSTUDY customers who are residing at the hostel may be affected.

Has the ASTUDY Standard Hostels Agreement enquiry been sent to the Level 2 Policy Helpdesk?

  • Yes, go to Step 7
  • No, refer the request to a Service Support Officer (SSO). Procedure ends here

7

Hostel Agreement outcome + Read more ...

Has the Level 2 Policy Helpdesk advised that the hostel agreement process has been completed?

  • Yes, the hostel or AHL:
    • agreed to all the conditions of the ABSTUDY Standard Hostels Agreement, go to Step 2 in Table 2
    • did not agree to all the conditions of the ABSTUDY Standard Hostels Agreement, go to Step 1 in Table 2
  • No:
    • advise the hostel/student/parent or carer that an outcome will not be reached until it has been confirmed that the hostel is a signatory to the ABSTUDY Standard Hostels Agreement
    • Contact the Level 2 Helpdesk for the current status
    • Procedure ends here

Payment of arrears and recording outcome

Table 2: This table describes the process involved when the Agreement has been returned, payment of arrears and recording the outcome.

Step

Action

1

Fortnight in arrears payment method + Read more ...

Where the hostel is not covered by a Standard Hostels Agreement, they must have payment of ABSTUDY allowances directed to the hostel via the normal fortnight in arrears payment method.

The student's record must be coded correctly to ensure ABSTUDY away from home allowances are directed to the hostel fortnight in arrears. See Processing an ABSTUDY claim for the correct coding.

Create a DOC on the hostel's organisation record. Include:

  • details of the contact with the hostel, and
  • that an Agreement will not be entered into by the hostel for the current 3 year period

Procedure ends here.

2

Recording outcome and review process + Read more ...

Display on Access DOC

Record details of the acceptance of the Agreement for the relevant 3 year period on a Display on Access DOC on the hostel's or AHL organisation record.

Scan a copy of the Agreement

Scan a copy of the ABSTUDY Standard Hostels Agreement (provided by the ABSTUDY and AIC Programme Team in their work allocation request) to the organisation record. Use the AHL organisation record for the Bilateral Hostels Agreement covering all AHL hostels.

Create a Review

Create a Review on the hostel's or AHL organisation record:

  • for 6 months prior to the end of the current 3 year period
  • to initiate contact with the hostel in relation to signing another Agreement for the following period

Update the record

Update the hostel organisation record, where required, with any changes to:

  • contact person
  • address
  • telephone
  • email
  • other

Procedure ends here.
