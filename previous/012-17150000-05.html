Quality control recovery of overpayments

Repay an overpaid amount to VAP

Table 1: This table describes the ways a health practitioner or facility can repay an overpaid amount to VAP.

Payment type

Description

Money orders and bank cheques

Accepting money orders and bank cheques

Money orders and bank cheques should be issued to Medicare Veterans' Treatment Benefits. Money orders/cheques issued to Medicare, Services Australia, Department of Veterans' Affairs or Veterans' Affairs Processing will also be accepted.

Repayment Cheque or Returned Cheque Advice

Processing Repayment Cheque or Returned Cheque Advice

The Department of Veterans' Affairs (DVA) will forward cheques to Medicare, attached to a Repayment Cheque or Returned Cheque Advice to action.

These cheques are processed the same as cheques received directly from a health practitioner or facility.

Electronic funds transfer (EFT)

Repaying an overpayment via EFT

Health practitioners or facilities can repay an overpayment by EFT using the EFT bank details for VAP.

When the Department of Veterans' Affairs (DVA) receives a payment via EFT it will be forwarded to Medicare and the site will be notified. These payments are processed the same as EFT payments received directly from a health practitioner or facility.
