Referral to a social worker + Read more ...

  • Check if a social worker at the service centre is available, to see if they can connect the customer immediately

Have these details ready:

  • Customer name
  • Customer Reference Number (CRN)
  • Payment type
  • Reason for referral. If the customer does not want to say, tell the social worker this before putting the call through
  • Get the customer's phone number in case the call drops out during transfer

If no social worker is available by the Social Work Line, go to Step 7.

Note: a referral under the multiple and complex needs category is made when a customer is unsupported, cannot be immediately referred by the Service Officer to a community agency and requires specialist intervention from an agency social worker. Customers in the category may experience a combination of vulnerabilities including mental health, personal crisis, homelessness, disability and complex caring issues. Inappropriate referrals will be returned to the Service Officer.
