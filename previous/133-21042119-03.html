Health professional enquiries

Table 1

Step

Action

1

Confirm enquiry + Read more ...

If the call is about:

  • an email telling them that they can no longer use Medicare Online to submit claims and data, go to Step 2
  • linking a service to their organisation PRODA account, go to Step 3
  • registering, activating or managing a Business to Business (B2B) device, go to Step 5
  • web service enquiries, go to Step 6
  • using other claiming channels, go to Step 7
  • escalating a PRODA issue, go to Step 8
  • finding a software developer, go to Step 9

2

Email that they can no longer use Medicare Online to submit claims and data + Read more ...

Tell the caller that they need to speak to their software developer.

If the caller:

  • does not have a software developer, go to Step 9
  • does not want to speak to a software developer, go to Step 6
  • asks about other claiming channels, go to Step 7

3

Link a service to organisation PRODA account + Read more ...

Ask the caller if they have their Minor ID.

  • Yes, go to Step 4
  • No, ask the caller to search for HW027 on Services Australia website and complete the form to register their Minor ID
    • They will need to call back when their minor ID has been registered
    • Procedure ends here

4

Select the service being linked + Read more ...

Select the relevant link and help the caller through the process:

  • Medicare Online (including ECLIPSE, DVA and AIR):
    • see Adding and linking to Medicare Online in PRODA (including ECLIPSE, DVA and AIR)
  • PBS Online:
    • see Adding and linking to PBS Online in PRODA
  • HPOS for PBS:
    • see Adding and linking to HPOS for PBS
  • HPOS for AIR:
    • see Adding and linking to HPOS for AIR
  • HPOS for Patient Verification (PV):
    • see Adding and linking to HPOS for Patient Verification
  • Aged Care web services:
    • see Setting up your web services software

5

Register, activate or manage a B2B device + Read more ...

To help a caller register, activate or manage Business to Business (B2B) devices in Provider Digital Access (PRODA), see Managing B2B Devices.

Procedure ends here.

6

Web service enquiries + Read more ...

If the caller has spoken to their software developer and are still concerned:

  • complete the Business Continuity Web Services Escalation Form for 'Healthcare locations'
  • escalate the call to eBusiness Service Centre (option 3)

Procedure ends here.

7

Other claiming channels + Read more ...

If the caller asks about other claiming channels, tell them to search for Online options for health professionals on Services Australia website.

They can:

  • use HPOS to submit claims, or
  • ask their patients to submit their own claims through:
    • Medicare in myGov, or
    • the Express Plus Medicare mobile app

Procedure ends here.

8

Escalate a PRODA issue + Read more ...

If the call is about escalating a PRODA linking issue, see Escalating organisation Provider Linking System incidents, complaints and feedback.

9

Find a software developer + Read more ...

If the caller wants to find a software developer, tell them to search for How to find a software developer on Services Australia website.

The page has a list of developers for Medicare Online and ECLIPSE.
