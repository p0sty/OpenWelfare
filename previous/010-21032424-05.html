Examples – Commencement following year 12 completion

Table 1

Item

Description

1

Student completes year 12 or equivalent study in the same year they commence their tertiary studies

Cathy is 19 and has completed the equivalent of year 12 studies at the end of Semester 1 2022.

Cathy enrolled in a TAP approved course commencing in Semester 2 2022. Cathy subsequently lodges a TAP online claim in July 2022.

Cathy is eligible for TAP, having:

  • commenced a tertiary course within 12 months immediately following completion of year 12 or equivalent, and
  • met all other eligibility criteria

2

Student completes year 12 or equivalent study in a different year from commencing their tertiary studies

Deb is 20 and has completed the equivalent of year 12 studies at the end of Semester 1 2021.

Deb enrolled in a TAP approved course commencing in Semester 1 2022. Deb subsequently lodges a TAP online claim in January 2022.

Deb is eligible for TAP, having:

  • commenced a tertiary course within 12 months immediately following completion of year 12 or equivalent, and
  • has met all other eligibility criteria

3

Student successfully completes year 12 twice

Nichole is 19 and will commence a TAP approved course in Semester 1 2022. Nichole lodges a TAP claim in January 2022.

In the TAP claim lodged, Nichole states:

  • successful completion of year 12 at secondary school at the end of Semester 2 2020, as well as
  • successful completion of the equivalent of year 12 studies at TAFE at the end of Semester 2 2021

Nichole is not eligible for TAP. Nichole is assessed on the first successful completion of year 12. In this instance this would be Nichole's secondary school studies completed at the end of Semester 2 2020.

4

Student has completed year 13 study

Coen is 19 and has lodged a TAP claim in January 2023 for a TAP approved course commencing Semester 1 2023.

Coen successfully completed Year 13 in a Tasmanian Government School at the end of Semester 2 2022.

Coen did not successfully complete Year 12 in 2021, however Year 13 study meets the requirements as an equivalent of a Year 12 course.

Coen is eligible for TAP, having commenced tertiary study within 12 months immediately following the first successful completion of a Year 12 equivalent course.

5

Student completed Year 12

Student commenced study semester 1 in the calendar year following completion of year 12 with a EFTSL 0.125 study load. Student relocates in semester 2 to commence full time study load of EFTSL 0.500. The students study load is EFTSL 0.625 for the first year of tertiary study. As the student does not meet the minimum 75 per cent full-time study load requirements over the 12 months from the date of commencement of their course, they are not eligible for TAP.
