Occupational Therapist quick reference

Details

Description

Speciality Code
  • 425 Occupational Therapist
  • 644 Occupational Therapist - Mental health (indicates FPS accreditation from OTA)

Location Type

Does the location falls under Section 19(2) or 19(5) direction?

  • Yes, follow the assessing and processing steps in Section 19(2) and 19(5) Directions
  • No, see Allied health and non-medical health professionals' provider number applications and requests

Eligible for Prescriber number

No

Request diagnostic imaging and pathology tests

No

Refer to Specialist or Consultant physician

No - referred specialist or consultant physician attendances will be non-referred attendance.

Medicare benefits are paid as Other non-referred attendance (Category 1, Group 2).

Eligible for 90 Day Cheque Scheme

No

Registration details
  • Current registration to practise occupational therapy with the Occupational Therapy Board of Australia. This is a board supported by the Australian Health Practitioner Regulation Agency (Ahpra)
  • Evidence of Ahpra registration is required for initial application
  • For subsequent applications, no further confirmation required if the Provider Director System (PDS) shows a registration status of Current
  • Renewal occurs 30 November

To be recognised to provide FPS, Services Australia receives an email notification of endorsement directly from Occupational Therapy Australia (OTA).
