FAQs for forms

Table 2

Item

Question

1

A valid MO022/MO026/MO027 is received and there is no claim registered. Should the claim be registered?

Yes. Due to arrival of correspondence, the claim needs to be registered and the history statement issued as part of same day processing (SDP).

2

An invalid MO022/MO026/MO027 is received and there is no claim registered. What steps should be taken?

Claim is to be registered as a “provisional claim”, and issue a letter requesting missing correspondence.

3

A MO026 (notice of past benefits request) is received for an existing claim. There are no funds on SAP and no notifications of a settlement or reimbursement. Should a notice to claimant be issued?

Yes, when the previous NOPB has expired Service Officers can issue a new notice to claimant.

4

An invalid MO026 is received and there is an existing claim. Should a letter be issued?

Yes, issue a Z2802.

5

An MO026 & MO021 are received, and there is an existing claim and authority on file. What action is to be taken?

Ensure that the previous authority is no long dealing with claim. Once confirmed, update.

6

An MO026 is received and there is evidence of a settlement or reimbursement arrangement. What action is required?

No change to actions - process claim.
