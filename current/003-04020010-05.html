This table provides the keywords that must be used when a claim is placed on hold.

Keyword

Description

AVTPOI

POI required

AVTRES

Residence assessment required

AVTQA

Quality assessment to be completed

AVTPPL

New claim waiting on Pre-payment letter to be returned by customer

AVTPE

Claims awaiting policy clarification

AVTEVI

Further evidence required

AVTMED

Medical evidence/death certificate required

AVTIA

Life Impact Assessment required

AVTRJ1

Claim is unsuccessful first rejection call attempt made and unsuccessful - follow up call required

AVTSVI

New secondary victim claim waiting for Secondary Victim Identification of all other Close Family Members

AVTINV

New secondary victim claim waiting on invite to claim responses for other secondary victims
