Additional card extensions

Applicants for permanent residency

(Flag P)

Linked to the Department of Home Affairs (DIAC link): ongoing for 42 months.

For enrolments over this period, manual extensions are required. (12 months from date of processing.)

Note: if a visa expires before the manual12 month extension period, the entitlement end date is the date the visa expires.

12 months

Linked to the Department of Home Affairs (DIAC link):

  • 1st card is issued for 12 months
  • 2nd card automatically reissues for 12 months
  • 3rd to 5th cards automatically issues for 6 months each
  • Note: 1st to 5th card = 42 months
  • After this time period they may be manually extended for 12 months at a time (from date of processing) if required

Not linked to the Department of Home Affairs:

  • 1st card is issued for 12 months
  • 2nd card is a manual extension for 12 months (from date of processing)
  • 3rd to 5th cards manual extension for 6 months each (from date of processing)

Refused applications:

  • Manual extension for 12 months at a time (from date of processing)

Applications for permanent protection visas (Flag Z)

Linked to the Department of Home Affairs (DIAC link): ongoing for 27 months.

For enrolments or an appeal to visa refusal over this period, a manual extension is required.

  • Note: if the visa expires before the 12 month extension period, the entitlement end date is the date the visa expires
  • Service Officers can access VEVO (visa entitlement) to confirm this

3 months

Linked to the Department of Home Affairs:

  • 1st card is issued for 3 months
  • 2nd card automatically reissues for 12 months
  • 3rd and 4th card issue is for 6 months each
  • Note: 1st to 4th card = 27 months
  • After 27 months, cards may be manually extended for 6 months at a time (from date of processing) if required

Not linked to the Department of Home Affairs:

  • 1st card is issued for 3 months
  • 2nd card is a manual extension for 12 months (from date of processing)
  • 3rd card onwards is a manual extension, for 6 months at a time (from date of processing)

Refused applications:

  • Manual extension for 12 months at a time (from date of processing)
