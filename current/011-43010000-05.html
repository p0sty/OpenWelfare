Table 8: when patient claims submitted for payment are over 2 years old from the date of services.

Patient claim…

Action

over 2 years old (less than 5 years old)

Medicare benefits less than $100 (paid for 1 claim for 1 Medicare card number)

Medicare benefit less than $100

Claim must be supported by an original or duplicate account/invoice or receipt for payment.

The claim can then be processed and paid without reference to culled history.

over 2 years old (less than 5 years old)

Medicare benefit more than $100 (paid for 1 claim for 1 Medicare card number)

Medicare benefit more than $100

Check the culled history for any duplicate payments. If:

  • there is no evidence of a duplicate payment on the culled history:
    • the claim can be processed and paid
  • if cannot be determined whether a previous payment exists:
    • If it is a manual paper claim, scan and email all the claim documents to MPS Assessing where it will be auto-uploaded to the electronic workflow (PaNDA) for assessment
    • If the work item is in the electronic workflow (PaNDA), then Proficiency Raise the claim for assessment

more than 5 years old

Service more than 5 years ago

All claims for services 5 years old or more and which:

  • were lodged manually at a Service Centre - the claim documents must be scanned and emailed to MPS Assessing, where they will be auto-uploaded to the electronic workflow (PaNDA) for assessment.
  • are within PaNDA- where the Service Officer cannot process the claim they should make sure that it is re-categorised to the Patient Claims over 5 years category for assessment

There is no charge incurred to Medicare for 5 year cull requests.
