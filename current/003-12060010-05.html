Fast Notes

Social workers need to use the following Fast Notes to document the claim determination:

  • GRANT or REJECT - CrP Perpetrator
  • GRANT or REJECT - LEAVING HOME Crisis Payment
  • GRANT or REJECT STAY IN HOME Crisis Payment
  • Crisis Payment Claim EXV/EXO Withdrawn

Retrospective address update

A customer may advise they have left an address that was not previously provided to Services Australia. Social workers can assess a CrP-EXV for an address not previously disclosed, however the social worker must include this address information in the Grant/Reject Fast Note, which includes:

  • the date the accommodation commenced
  • the date the accommodation ended
  • any rent paid and
  • the sharer’s details, where relevant

The Service Officer will be responsible for updating the address when finalising the CrP-EXV claim.

Contingency Fast Notes

Contingency Fast Notes are used in the following circumstances:

  • a Crisis Payment claim has been lodged using an old SU510
  • if Social Work Services Branch have advised activation of contingency arrangements

The contingency Fast Notes are:

  • CONTINGENCY Crisis Payment EXD GRA
  • CONTINGENCY Crisis Payment EXD REJ
  • CONTINGENCY Crisis Payment EXH GRA
  • CONTINGENCY Crisis Payment EXH REJ
  • CONTINGENCY Crisis Payment EXP GRA
  • CONTINGENCY Crisis Payment EXP REJ
