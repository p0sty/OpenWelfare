The tables contain the DEX/CPI status for different agreement countries and the United Kingdom.

Austria

Data Exchange (DEX)

Auto-indexation (CPI)

There is no data exchange process.

Each year in January, Austrian AGE/ INV/ SUR/ RSP/ SER/ WAR/ WID pensions are auto-indexed for customers residing in Australia.

Rate may be fixed percentage or sliding scale.

Belguim

Data Exchange (DEX)

Auto-indexation (CPI)

There is no data exchange process.

There is no auto-indexation of Belgian pensions.

Canada

Data Exchange (DEX)

Auto-indexation (CPI)

There is no data exchange process.

Each year on the first business day after 1 January, 1 April, 1 July and 1 October, Canadian AGE pensions are auto-indexed by a fixed percentage.

Each year on the first business day after 1 January, Canadian CPA/CPI/CPS pensions are auto-indexed by a fixed percentage.

Data Exchange (DEX)

Croatia

Data Exchange (DEX)

Auto-indexation (CPI)

Death data:

Each month the agency sends Croatia details of all Croatian pensioners in Australia who are known to have died in the past month.

Each month, Croatia sends the agency details of all Australian pensioners in Croatia who are known to have died in the past month.

Pension rates:

Each year in April, Croatia sends the agency details of the rate of Croatian AGE/INV/SUR pensions for customers in Australia.

There is no auto-indexation of Croation pensions.

United Kingdom

Data Exchange (DEX)

Auto-indexation (CPI)

Death data:

Each month, the agency sends the UK details of UK pensioners in Australia who are known to have died in the past month.

Pension rates:

Each week, the UK sends the agency details of new grants of UK AGE pension for customers in Australia.

Each 6 weeks, the UK send the agency details of UK AGE pension rate changes for customers in Australia.

Each year, the UK sends the agency details of all UK AGE pensions for customers in Australia.

Each year in May, the agency requests details from the UK of deceased persons and the rate of UK AGE pensions for customers in the UK.

Each year on the first Monday after 6 April, UK AGE/INV/WID/GPC pensions for customers in UK uprating countries are auto-indexed by a fixed percentage.
