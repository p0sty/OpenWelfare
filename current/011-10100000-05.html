Frequently asked questions about ODDs processing

Table 13

Item

Question and answer

1

Question: I have always lodged immunisation data this way, why the change?

Answer: The AIR have historically accepted data lodged via unofficial processes to assist with the transition from a childhood register to a whole of life register.

Lodging information using official processes captures all the required information. The AIR has up to date and accurate information available to all users.

2

Question: How should I send AIR this information?

Answer: Send information online via the AIR site, Practice Management Software or on an approved form, which is downloadable from the Services Australia website.

3

Question: But I don't have access to the AIR site

Answer: Services Australia can check AIR site access and help with any issues. Check access using the provider number.

4

Question: I can't go to the AIR site, I don't have internet access

Answer: If a Provider has a PRODA account, they can securely log on to the AIR site from any internet connected device, such as

  • a tablet
  • mobile phone
  • home computer

If the Provider has no internet access on any device, Service Officers can take details and forward to another area who will contact them to discuss how to assist. See Table 12

5

Question: I prefer to do business with you manually, via paper

Answer: Supporting electronic transmission of data to Services Australia aligns AIR with contemporary best practice across governments and leading private sector organisations such as banks, tax office etc. As well as being able to send immunisation data, using the AIR site provides many other benefits:

  • instantly update an AIR record
  • update an existing AIR record with amended information
  • record planned catch ups for overdue vaccines
  • view/print immunisation history statements
  • view/print payment statements and reconciliations
  • record a medical exemption (if an eligible provider type)

Identifying data lodgement methods

Table 14

Step

Action

1

Determine claim lodgement method

In AIR Mainframe:

  • Key 'YICE'
  • Key the provider number in the 'Prov Number' field, and select [Enter]
  • Key [F19] to return the registration details
  • Key [F14] to get to Provider Episode Details (YPES) screen

2

Assess Claim ID

The Provider Episode Details screen (YPES) lists data lodged (claims) to AIR by this provider. The Claim ID column provides information regarding the lodgement channel used (and therefore available) for each episode by assessing the last character of the Claim ID:

  • Claim ID with a ! or % means provider has lodged data using the AIR site
  • Claim ID with a + or @ means provider has lodged data using Practice Management Software
  • Claim ID with a $ means provider has lodged data using Practice Management Software that supports web services
  • Claim ID with a * or no symbol mean a claim that has been keyed by an AIR Service Officer (manual)
  • Claim ID with a & means a claim that has come through via an automatic upload
