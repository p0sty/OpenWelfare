Agreements and Arrangements

Services Australia deliver payments and services on behalf of other government agencies. A range of bilateral agreements and Bilateral Management Arrangements (BMA) governs our relationships with those agencies.

The Family Programmes and Strategic Partnerships Branch, in the Programme Design Group, provide strategic support for the Executive to help manage the relationships with the Department of Social Services (DSS), as well as those relationships with other departments.

For more information on:

  • the relationship with DSS, see Department of Social Services (DSS) Relationship Team page or email the Services Australia Relationship Team
