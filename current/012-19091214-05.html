Eligible for Prescriber number

Yes - for dentists and dental specialists only.

Request diagnostic imaging and pathology tests

Yes - dental practitioners are eligible to raise requests for their patients to a range of diagnostic imaging or pathology tests under Medicare

Refer to Specialist or Consultant physician
  • Dentists and dental specialists can refer to specialists only
  • Oral surgeons can refer to consultant physicians only

Eligible for 90 Day Cheque scheme

No

Registration details

Dental practitioner and Other Dental Practitioner

  • Current registration to practice with Dental Board of Australia. This is a board supported by the Australia Health Practitioner Regulation Agency (Ahpra)

Dental specialist

  • Evidence of Ahpra registration with:
    • Specialist recorded at Registration type, and
    • Specific specialty details in Specialty field. For example, periodontics

Renewal occurs 30 November.

Shell record

A shell record will be present in PDS to create a stem where an initial application for provider number is received.

Access to HPOS

Subsequent provider numbers are eligible to be issued via HPOS.
