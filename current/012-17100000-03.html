Lodge a DVA Webclaim

DVA Webclaim lodgement process

Table 1: This table describes the process of lodging a DVA Webclaim.

Stage

Action

Who

1

Health Professional Online Services (HPOS) portal + Read more ...

From Health Professional Online Services (HPOS) portal:

  • access DVA Webclaim homepage and select Make a claim
  • enter claiming data into the Make a Claim form
  • submit claim
  • claim is assessed in real time and is either:
    • paid
    • pended for further assessment

Health professional or delegate

2

Retrieve pended DVA Webclaims + Read more ...

Use transaction VQEI, XVZY, QP, DVW to retrieve DVA Webclaims that have been pended, where 'x' is the Service Officer state source office code.

Note:

'x' will be:

  • 'C' for Victoria (VIC)
  • 'G' for South Australia (SA)
  • 'I' for Western Australia (WA)

Stage

Action

Assesses information that displays against the relevant business rules relating to the restrictive situation.

Service Officer

Pay the claim or reject the service + Read more ...

Either:

  • key PI 42 to override the restriction and pay the claim
