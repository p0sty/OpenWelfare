This page contains information for Service Officers to process a voluntary recovery in the Compensation Case Management System (CCMS) so the amount will be deducted from the amount owing in the next notice of past benefits or notice of charge.

Processing a voluntary recovery

Add a voluntary recovery to a case

This table describes how to add a voluntary recovery to a case in the Ad-hoc recoveries work step in the Compensation Case Management System (CCMS).

Step

Action

1

Payment allocated and banked to correct case + Read more ...

To investigate a received payment, follow the steps in Accounts receivable and banking for Medicare Compensation Recovery to determine how it should be processed (if required).

Has the payment been allocated and banked to the correct case?

  • Yes, go to Step 2
  • No, the payment cannot be added to a case as a voluntary recovery. Investigate what case the payment is for and organise to allocate the payment to the correct case by completing the 'Allocation Change' email template. The Resources page contains a link to the email template
    • before releasing the work form, go to Step 6 to add a case note
    • procedure ends here

2

Purpose of payment + Read more ...

Has the purpose of the payment been confirmed?

  • Yes, go to Step 3
  • No, the payment cannot be added to a case as a voluntary recovery. Telephone the compensation payer to determine what the purpose of the payment is and take appropriate steps to resolve the claim
    • before releasing the work form, go to Step 6 to add a case note
    • procedure ends here

3

Coding - enter the voluntary recovery amount in CCMS + Read more ...

Scroll to the bottom of the Ad-hoc recoveries work form to finalise actions. The fields that must be completed are displayed in red.

Note: do not tab through any questions in the work form - double click or Press [Enter] on your numeric keyboard as instructed below.

Answer the questions in the Ad-hoc recoveries work item window as follows:

  • Refer case to supervisor? - Select No, Press [Enter]
  • Is this case a class action suit? - Select No, Press [Enter]
  • Verify claimantâ€™s address? Only select 'Yes' if Services Australia has been advised of a change of address (over the phone or in writing), or if other agency records have been updated with new address details. An Address field box will appear where the new address information can be entered. Select No, Press [Enter]
  • Updated Ad-hoc recovery amount? - Enter in the amount received in the 'Updated Ad-hoc recovery amount' field, Press [Enter]
  • Is it okay to revise the existing Ad-hoc amount? - Double click on 'Yes'. The work item window is rebuilt and the amount keyed moves from the 'Updated Ad-hoc recovery amount' field to the 'Existing Ad-hoc recoveries received' field

A system message is returned confirming the amount will be taken into account when producing the notice of past benefits or notice of charge, go to Step 4.

4

Suspend work item + Read more ...

Suspend this work item?

  • Yes, Press [Enter]. Do not release the work form, go to Step 6 to add a case note
  • No, go to Step 5

5

Go to next transaction + Read more ...

Is further action required on the case?

  • Yes, select which work queue from the drop down box that the case needs to go to for further action, then Press [Enter]. Do not release the work form yet, go to Step 6 to add a case note
  • No, do not select 'No' for both 'suspend' and 'go to next transaction', if this occurs the case will not suspend and will simply reactivate at the bottom of the current work queue

6

Add a case note + Read more ...

If the Case Notes screen has not opened automatically, select the Add notes button from the list on the right hand side of the main CCMS screen.

  • The top box shows all notes previously recorded on this case
  • In the blank bottom box, record the actions taken, information requested and from whom and reasons for a decision. Use standard case notes where possible
  • Select 'Add' to save notes. A confirmation box will appear, select 'Yes' to add case note
  • Press [F4] to release the work item, and
    • if this is the end of the process, case will suspend on CCMS, or
    • if further actions are required in other CCMS work queues, follow through on case until further actions are complete
