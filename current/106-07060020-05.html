Link Documents block

Link documents show the link between:

  • the applicant, and
  • the person signing the application

Record link documents when:

  • a parent/guardian or legally appointed custodian signs on behalf of the applicant
  • change of name

Valid link documents and codes are:

  • Australian Birth Certificate - LABC
  • Medicare Card - LBMC
  • Letter of Authority - LLOA
  • Proof of Parentage/Guardianship Documents - LLPG
  • Power of Attorney - LPOA
  • Doctors letter - LDRL
  • Foreign Birth Certificate - LOBC
  • Change of Name Document - LCON
  • Change of Name by Deed Poll - LCDP
  • Marriage Certificate - LMGS

3

Representative or Nominee's identity block

Representatives who sign behalf of the customers must provide:

  • identity documents
  • tax file number
  • link document

Record the nominee's identity documents in full in the relevant boxes:

  • document code
  • document number
  • place of issue
  • date of issue or expiry

Representative identity may not be the same as the customer.

Example: a parent signing on behalf of a child must meet the rules for a person 16 years and over. That is one primary and 2 secondary documents. The child under 16 provides one primary and one secondary document.

4

Application certification by Service Officer

  • Sign and print name in block letters, including phone number
  • Enter the CRN
  • Stamp 'Office stamp' box
  • Add within the 'notes' box
  • For a foreign passport, enter the visa number and sub class

5

Process if the document number is incorrectly or not recorded

The ATO systems will not process an application without a document number.

There is no real time check with other agencies to determine if the document number is correct at registration. .The ATO does post registration checks to verify the validity of the identity provided. A correct document number is essential for these checks.

The following message appears when incorrect information is added:

'The information you have provided does not match that held by DHA. Please check that the information you have entered is correct and try again'.

6

Service Officer signing as both referee and Service Officer on the NAT 1589

A Service Officer cannot sign as both referee and Service Officer on a NAT 1589.

Another Service Officer must accept and certify the form.
