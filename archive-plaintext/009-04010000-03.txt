This page contains information on how to help carers or correspondence
nominees who want to claim Carer Payment (CP) and/or Carer Allowance
(CA).

Select the relevant tab:

-   [] Self service tab explains how to help carers claim online
-   Assisted tab explains how to check a carer's circumstances to ensure
    a new claim is required, completing an Assisted Customer Claim (ACC)
    with the carer, and lodgement of paper forms
