This page contains information on child update screens. If updates are
required for Child Care Subsidy (CCS) purposes, refer to the relevant
CCS Operational Blueprint page for reference.

Staff trained in processing families claims in Process Direct, select
the Process Direct tab. Otherwise, select Customer First.
