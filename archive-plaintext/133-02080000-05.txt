Services available to a nominee via principal's record

Nominees wanting to access a Centrelink online account for their
principal(s) require their own active self service registration.

Payment Nominee (NOP)

Correspondence Nominee (NOC)

Payment and Correspondence Nominee (NOB)

The tables below describe which online services are available to each
type of nominee via a customer's record. The nominee types are described
above.
