Circumstances impacting compliance categories

The following categories are identified as circumstances impacting
compliance:

-   Accommodation/living arrangements
-   Behavioural issues
-   Caring responsibility
-   Cultural issues
-   Drug and alcohol dependence
-   Family relationships/Domestic violence
-   Financial issues
-   Geographic or social isolation
-   Job seeker in transition
-   Legal issues
-   Limited literacy/numeracy/English
-   Medical conditions - Cognitive/neurological
-   Medical conditions - Other illness/injury requiring frequent
    treatment
-   Medical conditions - Other illness/not resulting in a VI being
    recorded
-   Other
-   Recent prison release (in gaol 14 + days)
-   Technology Access
-   Transport issues
