Child support legislation

For a list of events that mean a customer is no longer eligible for a
child support assessment, see Child Support (Assessment) Act 1989.

-   section 12, Interpretation - happening of child support terminating
    events
-   For application to court by payer on the basis they are not the
    child's parent, see section 107, Declaration that a person should
    not be assessed in respect of the costs of the child
-   For effect of court order that specifies lump sum is in lieu of all
    future child support and all arrears owed, see, section 127, Effect
    of orders on administrative assessment of child support.
-   For application by payer that payee refund any child support
    received, section 143, Amounts paid where no liability to pay exists
    etc.
