Child Support online accounts

Child Support online accounts are a secure way for customers to manage
their payments and details at a time convenient to them.

A myGov account must be created before customers can link or access
Child Support online accounts.

Service Officers help customers by:

-   having access to the myGov Linking Application
-   issuing linking codes see Using the myGov Linking Application to
    link their Child Support online account to myGov
-   changing channel preferences for the receipt of letters, notices,
    statements and forms
