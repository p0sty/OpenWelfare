This page has the procedure to be used by Service Officers who are
conducting a Disbursement interview for customers exiting Voluntary
Income Management. Portions of this process must be completed by an
Income Management Contact Officer (IMCO).

On this page:

Disbursement interview for Voluntary Income Management

Using Voluntary IM Assessment and Disbursement workflows
