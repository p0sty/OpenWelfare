This document outlines the PBS for patients with CSU.

Chronic spontaneous urticaria and listing dates

Chronic spontaneous urticaria is a skin condition of persistent hives or
welts.

Listing dates: omalizumab - 1 September 2017

See Written Authority Required Drugs for more information.

Enquiries

Transfer enquiries about prescription arrangements to the Complex Drugs
Programs team and choose the option relevant to the condition treated.
