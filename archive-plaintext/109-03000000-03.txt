This page contains information to help Service Officers when:

-   customers make initial contact about a Centrelink decision
-   the Service Officer contacts a customer about an unfavourable
    decision

A separate procedure applies if the customer has already requested an
explanation, quality check or formal review of the decision.

On this page:

Service Officer contact guide

Customer has further information

Give the customer their internal review options

Customer has lodged an Explanation or formal review of a decision form
(SS351 or SY054)
