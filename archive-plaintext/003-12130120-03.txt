For Services Australia social workers only

This page contains the procedure for Services Australia social workers
to record Vulnerable Welfare Payment Recipient assessments in the Social
Work Recording Application (SWRA) and the Social Work Information System
(SWIS) in Process Direct and in Customer First.

On this page:

Recording VWPR assessments in the SWRA in Process Direct

Recording VWPR assessments in SWIS in Customer First

Recording VWPR Youth Exclusion (Detrimental) assessment in the SWRA in
Process Direct

Recording VWPR Youth Exclusion (Detrimental) assessment in SWIS in
Customer First

Recording Vulnerable Welfare Payment Recipient (VWPR) Youth Exclusion
(Not Practicable due to Support and Demonstrated Capacity) assessments
in the Social Work Information System (SWIS)
