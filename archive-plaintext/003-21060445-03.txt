Victoria (4-10 June 2021) - locating details in an online claim

The claiming period has ended for Victoria. Staff will only assess
online claims for this event that were lodged before the claiming period
ended on 2 July 2021.

Note: the extension of eligibility to include customers who were present
in a relevant area does not apply to this event.

Table 1: for claims lodged using a Victoria (4-10 June 2021) online
claim only. For help to progress any steps, contact Technical Peer
Support.
