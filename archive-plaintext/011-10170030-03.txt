This page details the processes relating to using the Child Immunisation
Summary guided procedure to check a child’s immunisation information for
Family Tax Benefit (FTB).

On this page:

View a child's AIR link status and immunisation status

Incorrect immunisation status

FTB Part A immunisation overrides for 2018-19 onwards

FTB Part A supplement immunisation overrides for 2012-13 to 2017-18

Secretary Exemptions for FTB part A for financial years 2018-2019
onwards and FTB Part A supplement for 2012-13 to 2017-18

Record and delete overrides for FTB part A for 2018-19 onwards and FTB
Part A supplement for 2012-13 to 2017-18
