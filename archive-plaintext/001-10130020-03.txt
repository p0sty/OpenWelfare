For Participation Solutions Team (PST)-skilled Service Officers or
Appeals Support Team Service Support Officer (priority reviews)

This page contains information about implementing the outcome of a
review of decision made under either the Targeted Compliance Framework
or the Job Seeker Compliance Framework.

|                                   | period.                           |
