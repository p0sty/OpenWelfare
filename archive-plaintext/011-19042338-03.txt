This page contains the processes Service Officers follow when enrolling
a newborn child in Medicare via CDMS and the Customer First Workload
Management system.

On this page:

Process newborn child enrolment (MOA) from CDMS Provisional Registration
screen

Process MAENROL documents

Medicare enrolment application form (MS004) – Part C – Enrolling a
newborn child

Process newborn enrolment to existing Medicare card in CDMS

Process newborn enrolment to their own Medicare card in CDMS

Process multiple births to existing Medicare card in CDMS

Transfer person to new or existing Medicare card in CDMS

Process newborn child enrolment for newborn with long name

Verifying documents in Document Verification System (DVS)

Process newborn child enrolment (MOA) from CDMS Provisional Registration screen
