This document outlines details of the PBS-subsidised brentuximab vedotin
and vorinostat for patients with cutaneous T-cell lymphoma (CTCL), and
brentuximab vedotin for patients with peripheral T-cell lymphoma (PTCL).

T-Cell lymphoma and listing dates

Peripheral T-cell lymphoma refers to a group of T-cell lymphomas that
develop away from the thymus or bone marrow.

Cutaneous T-cell lymphoma (CTCL) is a specific type of PTCL where the
cells attack the skin.

Listing dates are:

-   vorinostat - 1 July 2017
-    brentuximab vedotin - 1 April 2019 (CTCL)
-    brentuximab vedotin - 1 September 2021 (PTCL)

For more information, see Written Authority Required Drugs.
