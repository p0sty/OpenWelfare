This document outlines the PBS subsidised armodafinil and modafinil for
patients with narcolepsy (NARC).

Narcolepsy and listing dates

Narcolepsy (NARC) is a chronic neurological disorder of excessive
daytime sleepiness.

Listing dates:

-   modafinil - 1 April 2005
-   armodafinil - 1 November 2016

Enquiries

Transfer enquiries about prescription arrangements to the Complex Drugs
Programs team and choose the option relevant to the condition treated.
