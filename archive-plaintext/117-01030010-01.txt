This document outlines the actions to take when a customer's solicitor
or a compensation payer's solicitor asks about a specific case.

Release of information

Refer all requests to the Compensation Recovery Team (CRT).

Services Australia protects customer privacy. The agency does not
release protected information unrelated to a customer's compensation
claim to a:

-   compensation payer
-   compensation payers solicitor

The CRT can only send the below documents to compensation payers and in
some cases their legal representatives:

-   Preliminary Notice
-   Recovery Notice
-   Notice of release

The CRT will not release any information if there is any doubt.
