This page contains information to assist Participation Solutions Team
(PST) staff in actioning outstanding compliance action or applying
hardship provisions for job seekers affected by a natural disaster,
declared emergency, or assisting with a recovery effort through a
registered organisation when formal contingency arrangements have not
been invoked.

On this page:

Targeted Compliance Framework action during a natural disaster or
declared emergency when formal contingency arrangements have not been
invoked

Job Seeker Compliance Framework action during a natural disaster or
declared emergency for CDP participants when formal contingency
arrangements have not been invoked
