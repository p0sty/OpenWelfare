This page contains information on how to help a customer claim YA (job
seeker).

Select the relevant tab:

-   [] The Self service tab explains how to help a person make a claim
    online
-   The Assisted tab explains how to check a person's circumstances to
    make sure a new claim is required, complete an Assisted Customer
    Claim (ACC) with them, and lodgement of claims and documentation
