This document outlines details of the PBS-subsidised teduglutide for
patients with short bowel syndrome (SBS) with intestinal failure.

SBS with intestinal failure and listing dates

SBS with intestinal failure is a malabsorption disorder caused by a lack
of functional small intestine.

Listing dates:

-   teduglutide – 1 October 2019

See Written Authority Required Drugs for more information.
