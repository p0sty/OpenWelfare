This page contains information about how income support payment
customers can transfer to Age Pension.

Select the relevant tab:

-   [] Self-managed tab explains how to help customers complete the
    transfer online
-   Assisted tab explains how to identify if the customer should
    complete the transfer online and helping customers with enquiries
    about transferring to Age Pension
