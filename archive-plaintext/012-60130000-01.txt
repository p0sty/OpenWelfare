This document explains details about different types of call enquiries
in the Pharmaceutical Benefits Scheme (PBS).

General enquiries

General enquiries include:

-   routine queries that Service Officers should be able to answer or
    refer to other areas of Services Australia
-   policy related queries, as opposed to enquiries requiring clinical
    intervention

Pharmacist enquiries

Pharmacists can enquire about an authority by calling PBS General
enquires or PBS Authority approvals.

Service Officers contact Local Peer Support (LPS) if:

-   they cannot answer an enquiry, or need more detail to finish the
    enquiry
-   the call is about PBS Online Pharmacy payment

The Resources page contains links to contact details and tables listing
the main general enquiry types and PBS authority enquiry types.
