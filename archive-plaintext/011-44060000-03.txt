This page contains information about Reciprocal Health Care Agreement
(RHCA) eligibility for visitors to Australia and recording enrolment
details on the Consumer Directory Maintenance System (CDMS) for each
country.

Select the country from the drop down box to view the relevant RHCA
eligibility and enrolment information.
