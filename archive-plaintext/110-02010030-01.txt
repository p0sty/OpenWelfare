This document outlines how to assess and record details provided by
customers on the SA220 form. If a customer is partnered, they must
include their partner's income and assets details on the form where
requested.

Form issue

The SA220 form can be issued automatically as part of a bereavement
review. In some limited circumstances it may also be issued manually.

[]Customers can also complete bereavement reviews online.
