This document outlines how Service Officers help customers with the
progress of their claims. It includes the manual processing of claims.
It also includes claims processed incorrectly, and claim items over
$10,000.

Digital self service claiming

Customers can lodge Medicare claims using the:

-   Medicare online account, or
-   Express Plus Medicare mobile app

The processing of claims can take up to 7 days (from date of
submission). Escalated claims, or claims needing further investigation,
can take longer.

Note: for claim items over $10,000, see Claiming Medicare benefits
online.
