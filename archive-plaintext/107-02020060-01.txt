This document outlines the procedure for adding a new record for an
institution in the Debt Management and Information System (DMIS).

Organisation debts

Organisations may have the following debts:

-   ABSTUDY
-   Assistance for Isolated Children (AIC) Scheme
-   Parental Leave Pay (PPL)
-   Family Tax Benefit (FTB) payments to an Approved Care Organisation
    (ACO)
-   Assurance of Support (AOS) payment where the assurer is an
    organisation
-   Third Party Organisation (TPO) payments

Raise a debt against the organisation if they directly received the
payment.

ABSTUDY or Assistance for Isolated Children (AIC) Scheme

Raise the debt against an educational institution if the ABSTUDY or AIC
scheme student is no longer qualified for:

-   an education allowance or living allowance, and
-   the institution directly received the allowance

Boarding institutions

Boarding institutions are responsible for overpayment of:

-   Pharmaceutical Allowance (PhA)
-   Rent Assistance (RA)
-   Remote Area Allowance (RAA
-   Living Allowance paid fortnightly
-   Term-in-advance, as an ABSTUDY boarding instalment or on account to
    the boarding school, hostel or residential college
-   Under-16 Boarding Supplement. This payment ceased on 1 January 2019
-   Additional Assistance paid to the boarding institution

Schools

Schools are responsible for overpayments of:

-   School Fees Allowance
-   Additional Assistance paid to the school

Tertiary institutions

Tertiary education institutions are responsible for overpayments of:

-   Away-from-base assistance paid as residential costs and fares to the
    tertiary education institution
-   Additional Assistance paid to the tertiary education institution
-   Lawful Custody Allowance if paid to the tertiary education
    institution
-   Payments to the institution made under the Residential Costs Option
    for board and lodging when student was not eligible
-   Student Contribution (previously called Higher Education
    Contribution Scheme (HECS) fees)
-   Course fees where paid to the tertiary education institution

This page contains information on how to create a new institution debt
record on the Debt Management and Information System (DMIS).
