This procedure only relates to the 2000-01 reconciliation financial year

This document outlines how to assess a customer's entitlement to the
$1,000 waiver for Family Tax Benefit (FTB) debts for the 2000-01
financial year.

Manual check to test eligibility for the $1,000 transitional waiver

Some family assistance debts for the 2000-01 financial year did not have
an MFU activity created. These debts require manual checking to test the
customer’s entitlement to the $1,000 transitional waiver.
